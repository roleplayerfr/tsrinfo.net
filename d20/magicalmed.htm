This comprehensive 144-page book answers many of the questions previously overlooked by gaming supplements. With informative text, generation systems, worksheets, and appendices. A Magical Medieval Society: Western Europe allows GMs and players to export the feel of medieval times without being tied to the history.

This work includes:

Manors: How they work and how to generate them for your campaign. Artistocrats: How many in your kingdom, their income, and their lands
Cities: How they function and exhaustive guidelines for their creation. Buildings: How much and how long it takes to build just about anything you need.
Churches: How multiple sources of religion affect the medieval mindset.Economies: The best complex economic simulator for D20 ever created.

A Magical Medieval Society: Western Europe puts the wealth and depth of European demographics in your hands. It allows you to create your world the way you've always wanted it to be!

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons(R), Third Edition Core Books, published by Wizards of the Coast, Inc
