Title: Dungeon Crawl Classics #6: Temple of the Dragon Cult
Type: Adventure
For: 4-6 character, levels 8-10
Author: John Seavey
Published: 2004
Publisher: Goodman Games
Format: 32-page book
Remember the good old days, when adventures were underground, NPCs were there to be killed, and the finale of every dungeon was a dragon on the 20th level? Those days are back. Dungeon Crawl Classics adventures don't waste your time with long-winded speeches, weird campaign settings, or NPCs who aren't meant to be killed. Each adventure is 100% good, solid dungeon crawl, with the monsters you know, the traps you remember, and the secret doors you know are there somewhere.

In Temple of the Dragon Cult, the characters are called in to pursue a dragon that the king's army was able to wound but not kill. It seems straightfoward enough: The army tracked the dragon to its lair, and the only thing the characters have to do is go in and kill it. But this dragon has a devoted cult of dragonblood followers who worship its every breath. Its lair is their temple - and they'll fight to the death to defend their dragon-god...

If you enjoy this adventure, look for the rest of the Dungeon Crawl Classics series!
