REQUIRES THE USE OF THE DUNGEONS & DRAGONS PLAYER'S HANDBOOK, THIRD EDITION, PUBLISHED BY WIZARDS OF THE COAST.

Sword and Sorcery books are published under the Open Game License and are 100% compatible with 3rd edition rules.

The ratmen - or, as they call themselves, the slitheren - are some of the deadliest foes adventurers can face: foul humanoid rodents, intent on spreading evil throughout the lands of men, elves, and dwarves. In the dank reachers of the Mourning Marshes, the many broods of ratmen have come together in a profane alliance, intent on mayhem and the downfall of all other races.
Vigil Watch: Warrens of the Ratmen includes a wealth of information about the ratmen, their culture, society, and biology. Here, you will read of their diabolical plots and schemes, of the twisted monsters that lurk in the deadly marshlands. Here, too, is information on new broods of slitheren - the sorcerous Black Pelts, the powerful Storm Chasers, the cunning Forge Crawlers, and the enigmatic Unseeing, as well as rules for developing slitheren as player characters. Two detailed ratman settlements come ready-made for adventuring.

Join the struggle - meet the slitheren and their minions face to face. And pray that you live to tell the tale.
