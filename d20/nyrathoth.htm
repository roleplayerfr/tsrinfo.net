Item Code: #GHS1004
Title: The Plague of Nyrathoth
Type: Adventure
For: 6th level characters
Author: Gabe Ivan
Published: 2002
Publisher: Guildhouse Games
Format: 32-page book
It has been sealed for centuries.

Hundreds died slow, wasting deaths within its unforgiving walls. Their lives were sacrificed to pursue Nyrathoth's dream of an apocalypse plague capable of destroying the world. The wizards of Vundara sealed it to protect the world, after ending Nyrathoth's depraved experiments.

Now, a reckless adventurer has broken the seals on the plague pits of Nyrathoth in search of treasure. He left with more than gold. Nyrathoth's dark dream may yet come to fruition. Does your party have the courage to seek a cure in the pits, or will they simply add their numbers to the death toll?

The Plague of Nyrathoth is an adaptable adventure for the d20 system for 6th level characters.

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons
Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
