Title: Spellbound: A Codex of Ritual Magic
Type: Accessory

With circle complete, and scroll in hand, the old man prepared himself for the arduous casting. With his skill he could level mountains, raise armies, or call forth untold power, but at what risk when one misspoken syllable could mean death... or worse!

  • Over 150 fully detailed rituals, ranging from simple to epic
  • An entirely new form of power to be wielded by any arcane or divine caster of any level
  • Ritual skills, feats, magic items, and prestige classes
  • A complete system of magic suitable for any campaign setting
