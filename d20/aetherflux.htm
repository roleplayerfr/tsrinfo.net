Title: Aether & Flux: Sailing the Traverse
Type: Accessory
Author: Steven Grover, Brian Moseley & Valorie Grover
Published: 2003

Swashbuckling Adventures in Fantasy Space!

Beyond the clouds, wonders await those who rise to meet the call. As sailing ships and elven ormralest embark into the traversal frontier, heroes' lives are forever changed. Spacefarers come for hold or glory, and the traverse stands ready to test their mettle. From the seedy port of Maythorne's Wheel to the deadly realm of Lyriah, a great ocean lies amongt the stars.

Sailing the Traverse contain everything needed for adventures in fantasy space, including:
  • New spacefaring spells, feats, and items.
  • Over 30 classic and fantastic ship designs.
  • Complete deckplans of eight aetherships, and the harbor of Maythorne's Wheel.
  • Three traversal ages, from Emergence through Exploration to the climatic Empire.
Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast
