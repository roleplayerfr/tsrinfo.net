Take Your Game to a New Level

The Advanced Player's Guide is a companion volume to the Player's Handbook and other d20 core rulebooks which provides a wealth of new rules options for players and Game Masters. New and expanded classes, new skills, new combat systems, new magic systems and more - all the tools you need to superchrafe your campaign!

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, published by Wizards of the Coast.
This product utilizes updated material from the v.3.5 revision.

Sword and Sorcery books are published under the Open Game License and are 100% compatible with v.3.5 rules.
