GYGAXIAN FANTASY WORLDS VOLUME IV
Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons, Players Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.

The ultimate book of names. With over 100,000 names, name generators and more this source book is a must have for any game designer and writer. A veritable host of nationalities and cultures are covered from Indian, Korean and Mongol to Aztec and Mayan. From Medieval French to Spanish, from the fantastic to the mundane, from Italian to Polynesian and more this extraordinary collection covers it all. For the Lejendary Adventure and d20 enthusiasts a new class, the Onomancer, but more a complete name generating system.

A book by any other name is not
Gary Gygax's Extraordinary Book of Names!
