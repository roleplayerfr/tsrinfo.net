The Dedicated & The Devoted

Some pursue adventure as a means to glory and riches... while others battle chaos and evil in dedication to a higher cause. Whether they take up sword and shield for a righteous cause or master physical arts to protect body, mind and spirit, these champions are anything but ordinary.
