A HERO'S COMPANION

Every hero needs a good companion, and the Blue Rose Companion complements and expands the material from Blue Rose: The Roleplaying Game of Romantic Fantasy. It expands options for hero creation and improvement, offers new insights into the arcane mysteries, and provides new opponents for your heroes to overcome. In its pages you will find:

  • Expanded Paths: Heroic paths for all three roles to take you heroes from 1st all the way to 20th level!
  • New Feats: Options ranging from Estates and Leadership to Elemental Pacts and mastery of arcane secrets.
  • New Arcana: Give your heroes the power to cloud minds, enter the world of dreams, or command the most powerful forces of nature!
  • Arcane Rituals: Use rituals to increase your heroes' arcane power and reach far beyond the arcana of a single individual.
  • Summoning: Master the arts of summoning elementals to aid your adept or, if you dare, plumb the secrets of calling darkfiends from the depths of Shadow.
  • Places of Power: Seek out the wellsprings of mystic might in the land and protect them from the forces of evil that seek to corrupt them and turn them toward their own ends.
  • Arcane Items: Infuse items with arcane power and wield fantastic items like seer stones, blessed blades, hawk-bows, and more in the cause of the Light.
  • Expanded Bestiary: Over two-dozen new foes for your heroes to overcome, plus templates for modifying existing creatures to create entirely new ones!
The Blue Rose Companion gives you the tools you need to tell all-new stories and live exciting new adventures in the fantastic world of Aldea. Don't go adventuring without you companion!
