Title: A Mage's Tale

Author: Gabe Ivan
Published: 2002

Tharilorn the Wise
Lyn Ta'nhar, the Shaker of Mountains
The Plague Lord, Nyrathoth

These are but a few of the legendary wizards who learned their art in the halls of Vundara. Some were champions of the weak and oppressed. Some were heralds of doom and despair. Some were virtually forces of nature unto themselves. All were undisputed masters of the arcane, and it is the hope of joining their ranks that draws students in droves to the wizard-city.

Vundara, the city of magic, rumored to be the birthplace of wizardry, rests in the foothills of the Faeforn Mountains. It is here that legend claims the titans taught magic to the young race of elves. The art they learned eventually crafted the towering spires and marble halls of Vundara. Labyrinthine libraries and ancient tomes hold the true power of the city: knowledge. The magic houses vie for prestige and influence. The games they play are acts of subtlety and spectacle that strike awe in the uninitiated. One must master both the art and the politics of wizardry to take a place among legends.

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
