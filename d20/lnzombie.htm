Title: The Lords of the Night: Zombies
Type: Accessory
Author: Andrew Kenrick and Stuart 'Karis' Renton
Published: 2004
Publisher: Bottled Imp Games
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
Something stirs in the darkness of the mortal realms...

Where zombies were previously nothing more than mindless, shambling creatures, that is no longer the case.

Something has changed...

From the depraved minds of the greatest alchemists, surgeons and necromancers come the Risen: undead beings with great power and a terrible hunger for the flesh of the living.

This book presents six templates for the creation of the Risen, plus a horde of creatures to challenge and terrify your players. Create powerful characters or use this book as a collection of the most horrifying monsters imaginable. Whatever you decide, one thing is certain:

THE NIGHT WILL NEVER BE THE SAME AGAIN

Requires the use of the Dungeons and Dragons Player's Handbook produced by Wizards of the Coast
