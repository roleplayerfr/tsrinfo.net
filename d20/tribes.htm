They say that there's strength in numbers.
Now you can see for sure!

This Counter Pack, Tribes, Clans & Cults contain over 300 counters for use with any tabletop fantasy roleplaying game! From the orcish might of Mogor's Fist to the cruel devotion of the Cult of Bakaal, your players will be able to see what they're up against in full color.

This Counter Pack includes images for:
- Mogor's Fist, a powerful orc strike force
- Cult of Bakaal, a group of evil demon-worshippers
- Pax Hobgoblinica, a powerful hobgoblin military force
- Bertram's Guild, a seedy urban organization
- Knights of Solorien, a shining example of bravery and justice
- The Vampire's Legion, the living dead searching for victims
- The Prince's Worgriders, various shades of goblin power
- The Jirockk Clan, fire giants with an attitude
- Residents of the Mattadin, the inhabitants of a fey valley

This Counter Pack features every counter necessary for use with the 72-page Fiery Dragon adventure:
BEYOND ALL REASON

Includes new group feats by Mark Clover of Creative Mountain Games!
