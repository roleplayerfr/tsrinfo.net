Item Code: #MKY1101
Title: Tales From the Blood Plateau: Caravan of Hope
Type: Adventure
For: 3rd-4th level characters
Author: David K. Hurd
Published: 2001
Publisher: MonkeyGod Enterprises
Format: 28-page book
A caravan of mercy is in need of some worthy adventurers to accompany it to the beleagured elves of Northwood.

Easy enough, right? Wrong!

The trail strays across the dreaded Blood Plateau. On the way, our heroes must overcome assassination attempts, baby stealing goblins, and a tribe of blood thirsty orcs. All this only to discover they have been duped into helping out a pair of drug smugglers.
