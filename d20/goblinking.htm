Title: Slave Pits of the Goblin King
Type: Adventure
For: 4-6 characters, levels 5-9
Author: James M. Ward
Published: 2001
Publisher: Fast Forward Entertainment
Format: 48-page book
Black-scaled goblins in a valley of death!
The human frontier town Rothyra sits on a low hilltop deep in the monstrous southern wilderness. Powerful adventurers reach out from here to slay beasts and accumulate treasure while patrols keep the worst of the Green Races at a distance. But now, unusual raider come up from the south, scaled goblins magically changed by drinking dragon blood. Lashing human slaves before them they head back south to their mysterious valley.

A Green Races Aventure!
Goblins, trolls, ogres, and their filthy lot are collectively known among humans as the Green Races. A Green Races adventure takes place far beyond the protection of walled human cities, their armies and justice, out in lands where fortunes are made with steel and sorcery!

Slave Pits of the Goblin Kings includes:
Preliminary encounters in and around the frontier town Rothyra, including an unexplored ruin and a DragonGoblin raid.
Complete maps and descriptions of the Green Dragon Inn, the deadly valley and all the encounters there.
Many new monsters, including DragonGoblins, their strange arachnids and plant pods, plus the splintered and dangerous wood golem

Visit www.fastforwardgames.com to get even more aventure, including a preliminary adventure where players must rescue a captured noble maiden, a child pivotal to ancient human prophecy.

Slave Pits of the Goblin King is a d20 System adventure for 4 to 6 characters of levels 5 to 9. It is the third of a trilogy of Green Races adventures that began with Fortress of the Ogre Chieftain and Temple of the Troll God.

About the Author:
James M. Ward is the award-winning creator of Metamorposis Alpha and Gamma World, for many years the director of the entire design department at TSR responsible for all Dungeons & Dragons.
Assisted by Lester Smith and Timothy Brown, he now creates a new fantasy universe, with monstrous Green Races seeking power on the southern frontier of elven and BronzeMen civilization - the world of DragonElves.
