It's Not Easy Being... Undead!
There comes a time in every adventurer's life when he faces the pure, unadulterated evil of undeath. Some are able to overcome their fears to face that evil unflinchingly, while others flee in terror. But not all of those that are able to stand up against that evil survive. So what happens to those heroes slain by the undead? If they're lucky, their friends cremate them, powder their bones and sprinkle holy water over their ashes before scattering them to the winds. But when they don't, the slain hero must suddenly cope with the knowledge that what he does in undeath is evil beyond measure.

Think You Know How to Beat These Baddies?
A skeleton is a skeleton and a ghost is a ghost, right? Not anymore. Many undead creatures are unthinking, unfeeling abominations whose only purposes are to kill and consume the living. But some are super-intelligent beings, powerful beyond words, with nothing short of complete domination as their goals. Every one of these creatures is unique, with their own powers and their own innate abilities. With this book in your hands, you won't have to use those stock creatures any more!

New Experiences for a New Generation!
Whether you're a new player or someone who's been role-playing for decades, this book has something for you. Some will want to take on the challenge of playing the undead, while others will immediately take to eradicating the evil plague of undeath. Either way, prepare for the kind of campaign you've never experienced before!

The Complete Monstrous Undead Compendium is a d20 System sourcebook designed for players and gamemasters that are tired of the same-old, same-old and want to try something more than a little different. Included within these pages are more than thirty new races and racial templates, along with nine new prestige classes and a host of other tools you can use to build characters who'll fight on both sides of death's curtain!

  • 5 undead races
  • 25 mortal and undead racial templates
  • 9 prestige classes
  • 37 new arcane and divine spells
  • 14 new artifacts and magical items

Beas, Bret Boyd, Jarad Fennell, B.D. Flory, Tim Kidwell, Todd Laing, M.K. McArtor, Gary McBride, W.
