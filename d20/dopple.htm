Title: The Complete Guide to Dopplegangers
Type: Accessory
Author: Keith Baker
Published: 2002
Publisher: Goodman Games

What if everyone around you were a doppleganger... and only you didn't know?

The Complete Guide to Dopplegangers is a stand-alone, world-neutral sourcebook covering everything you ever wanted to know about dopplegangers. It includes:

  • The true origin of these bizarre creatures.
  • Background on doppleganger social structure, including their various life stages and their relationship to mimics.
  • Details on how the dopplegangers have penetrated human society.
  • New feats and skills for dopplegangers, plus the dreaded Protean Warrior class.
  • Unique campaign ideas centered around dopplegangers.
  • New monster stats for intriguing variations on the standard doppleganger.
  • Supplemental material online.
The Complete Guide to Dopplegangers is the third volume in the Complete Guide series. Each Complete Guide is exactly what it sounds like; a complete guide to playing a given kind of monster. As a GM, you'll learn how to run that monster, both in combat and role-playing situations. And since every Complete Guide includes guidelines on playing the monster as a character race, players have new options, too.

The Complete Guide to Dopplegangers can be inserted easily into any fantasy campaign.
