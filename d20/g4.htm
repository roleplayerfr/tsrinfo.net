The Hunt for a Legendary Treasure

Deep beneath a peaceful valley lies the vault of the legendary drow adventurer Larin Karr. Rumor has it Karr has gone, but his vast treasure, acquired from years of plundering hordes in the Underdark, still remains. Can you find the keys to his impenetrable vault?

A Mini-Campaign Setting

The Vault of Larin Karr details Quail Valley, its residents and monsters, and the twisting tunnels of the Underdark that stretch beneath it. The Vault of Larin Karr takes PCs from 4th to 9th level, during which time they fight dragons, find a missing statue for a band of renegade elves, save the village of Pembrose from scheming hobgoblins, and explore the Underdark and its many dungeons - including the vault itself! Contains new monsters and magic items!

A d20 adventure for 4 to 6 characters of 4th to 9th level
