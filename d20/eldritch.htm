Item Code: #WW16100
Title: The Book of Eldritch Might
Type: Accessory
Author: Monte Cook
Published: 2001
Publisher: Malhavoc Press / Sword & Sorcery Studios
Format: 48-page book
Arcane spellcasters call power into themselves to serve their every wish. Harness their magic with this d20 sourcebook for all things arcane. Inside you'll find:

Eleven original feats, including the new eldritch type, which grant specific magical abilities to enhance your spells in ways that metamagic feats can't.
Three new prestige classes: the embermage (the supreme fire mage), the graven one (whose body is covered in magical tatoos), and the mirror master (specializing in unique mirror magic).
More than 60 new spells of all levels that you can add to any campaign.
A like number of magic items of all types, from weapons and wondrous items to artifacts and even magic poisons.
A new monster template, the magical construct, which allows any spellcaster to create golemlike guardians and servants based on existing monsters.

Malhavoc Press is Monte Cook's d20 System imprint devoted to publishing unusual magic, monsters, and evocative elements that go beyond traditional fantasy. Malhavoc Press products exhibit the mastery of the d20 System rules that only one of the game's original designers can offer.
