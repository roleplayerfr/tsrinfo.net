The Continent of Ghelspad
Playground of titans, battlefield of gods, land of empires and magic: Ghelspad is a vivid and exciting setting for your fantasy campaign. This volume details the lands, peoples, cultures, gods, and history of Ghelspad, from the ancient titanic epochs to the horrors of the Divine War and the tumultuous recent years, when the empire of Calastia has risen to threaten the land's freedom, and titanspawn continue to plot the return of their evil masters.

Scarred Lands Campaign Setting: Ghelspad provides Game Masters with a fully developed setting ready-made for adventure. Enter the continent of Ghelspad and experience the world of the Scarred Lands first-hand.
