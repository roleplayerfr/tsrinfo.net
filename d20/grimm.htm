The sun dips behind the clouds, and a cold wind picks up. The leaves begin to shiver, you do too... you've strayed from the path, my dear, and we're oh so glad to welcome you.

Twisted Tales, Hateful Foes

Explore a world of dark and twisted fairy tales in this self-contained RPG. The world is familiar, but one step removed from the stories we learned as children, and the changes aren't for the better.

Play iconic kids like the bully, the nerd, the dreamer, and the jock... all trying to get home.

Use guile and imagination to avoid a messy death at the hands of an ogre's club, a wolf's jaws, a witch's spells, or an evil step-mothers's poisons.

Find incantations to master in a warlock's tower, discover your true heritage as a prince or princess of the realm, befriend the talking animals of the forest... or just plain stay alive.
