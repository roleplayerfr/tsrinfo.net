Title: Cities of Fantasy: Stonebridge - City of Illusion
Type: Accessory
Author: Patrick Younts
Published: 2003
Publisher: Mongoose Publishing
Format: 64-page perfect-bound book
If there were a place where the written word was priceless, where a painting or a sculpture was worth more than diamonds and where a song could inspire a love that lasts for centuries, would you go there?

If there existed a city where genius and beauty were one and the same, where invention and ambition were prized above martial skill and where dragons and mortals lived together in eternal harmony, would you visit there?

If there existed such a city where magic in service to beauty was the highest ideal and where illusions revealed the greatest truths, would you live there?

And if you came to such a place, could you ever leave again?

Stonebridge is a city like no other, an oasis of renaissance culture, home to art obsessed copper dragons and an impassioned race of gnomes whose blood sings with the twin fires of magic and dragon spirit. Stonebridge is home to the greatest geniuses of the age, men and women who have seized greatness and embraced innovation, who have slipped the boundaries of conventional thought for the endless vistas of imagination.

Inside you will find:

History and the Present Day

Palazzo, Piazzo and Sandstone Streets

Vineyard, Farm and Villa

Dragons, Dreamers and Builders

Stonebridge Adventures

Beasts, Magic and Wondrous Machines

FOR GAMES MASTERS AND PLAYERS ALIKE

Originally solicited under the title Arcania - City of Magic and later under the title Stonebridge - City of Gnomes.
