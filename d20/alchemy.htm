The Thirst for Knowledge!

Looking for the secrets of turning lead into gold, crafting new and more-powerful magic items, or becoming a master of your craft? Are you seeking better healing or more firepower for those long dungeon treks?

Alchemy & Herbalists provides all of the essential details for bringing these two important skills into sharp focus for you campaign. Whether you're seeking to expand the abilities of your character or or the boundaries of your world, this tome holds the knowledge you seek!

This product requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
