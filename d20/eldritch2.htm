Title: Book of Eldritch Might II: Songs and Souls of Power
Type: Accessory
Author: Monte Cook
Published: 2002
Publisher: Malhavoc Press
Format: 72-page book
The power of Eldritch Might is yours to command!

Summon new and exciting sources of powre with this d20 sourcebook, packed with all things arcane for characters of any level. Features include:

  • Completely redesigned versions of the sorcerer and bard classes, including rules for music-based bardic magic.
  • More than 100 new spells and spellsongs.
  • Rules for using soul magic, a new type of arcane power involving sentient spells.
  • Over three dozen new feats, prestige classes, and magical items.
  • All-new magical monsters, ready to drop into your campaign.
Malhavoc Press is game designer Monte Cook's d20 System imprint devoted to publishing unusual magic, monsters, and evocative elements that go beyond traditional fantasy. Malhavoc Press products exhibit the mastery of the d20 System rules that only one of the game's original designers can offer.
