Avenge a bloody betrayal!
The vast ogre strongholds lie well south of human lands. Centuries past, humans thought to keep the ogres at bay by building a mighty fortress along their frontier, but they were betrayed and slaughtered in their new keep by ogres who had tunneled their way in from below. Now the fortress is haunted by dead human and ogre warriors who protect the most magical treasures anywhere in the reams of the Green Races.

A Green Races Adventure!
Goblins, trolls, ogres, and their filthy lot are collectively known among humans as the Green Races. A Green Races adventure takes place far beyond the protection of walled human cities, their armies and justice, out in lands where fortunes are made with steel and sorcery!

Fortress of the Ogre Chieftain includes:
Four preliminary encounters, compelling tales prior to the haunted fortress.
Complete maps and descriptions of the fortress with its many towers and denizens.
Many new monsters, including spirit and ghost dragons, both goblin and ogre chieftains and shamans, and several fungi and molds to vex the adventurous.

Visit www.fastforwardgames.com to get even more adventure, including a preliminary adventure where players are monsters setting the stage a century in the past, plus full color versions of every map in the adventure!

Fortress of the Ogre Chieftain is a d20 System adventure for 4 to 6 characters of level 3 to 5. It is the first of a trilogy of Green Races adventures that continues with Temple of the Troll God and Slave Pits of the Goblin King.

About the Author:
Timothy Brown is the award-winning creator of the Dark Sun universe and the popular Pulp Dungeons adventures, for many years the manager of the Ravenloft, Spelljammer, and Planescape worlds. Assisted by Lester Smith, award-winning designer of Dark Conspiracy and Dragon Dice, he now brings a new fantasy universe to life, one where Green Races roil on the fringes of civilization and wyrms seek an unholy alliance with elves - the world of DragonElves.
