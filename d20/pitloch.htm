Item Code: #MYG0002
Title: The Pit of Loch-Durnan
Type: Adventure
For: 4-6 characters, levels 2-4
Author: Doug Herring and Andrew Thompson
Published: 2001
Publisher: Mystic Eye Games
Format: 72-page perfect-bound book
Loch-Durnan, a forested town shrouded in mist. It is a town with closely guarded secrets, a town in trouble. Goblins have occupied the caverns of "the pit," a hidden gold mine that makes the small town prosperous. Injured in a raid on the goblins, the town's twin brother mayors seek adventurers to do what they cannot. Simply put, drive the goblins from the mine. Seems easy enough for a band of adventurers, but not all is as it seems in the sleepy, mist-shrouded town. The mine is not their only secret.

This massive adventure from Mystic Eye Games will fit into any d20 fantasy setting. It is ideal for 4-6 characters from 2nd to 4th level. Immerse yourself in a plot of intrigue, betrayal, and murder while trying to help a small town rid its mine of evil goblins and more.
Can your band of adventurers help Loch-Durnan, or will they die in the dark caverns of the pit?

Requires the use of Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, Published by Wizards of the Coast.
