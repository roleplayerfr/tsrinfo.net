Welcome to the World of Loerem

The fantasy epic of Sovereign Stone returns in a new edition! Rediscover the world conceived by renowned fantasy artist Larry Elmore and popularized by Margaret Weis & Tracy Hickman in the best-selling Sovereign Stone novel trilogy from HarperCollins.

Sovereign Stone Classic is a complete campaign setting for the d20 system, updated to version 3.5 rules. Everything uniquely Sovereign Stone has been revised and expanded. The already distinctive races (seafaring orks, nomadic marauder dwarves, feudal Japan-like elves, a half-dozen standout human cultures) are further highlighted with information compiled from several published sourcebooks. The critically acclaimed magic system makes spellcasting uncertain and enthralling - now with complete rules for designing your own spells! The character classes have been upgraded with new feats, skills, and task resolutions. Joining the many innovative features in Sovereign Stone Classic are Destiny Points, which combines role-playing rewards with high drama to encourage and inspire truly heroic actions!

Sovereign Stone Classic is a 224-page clear, concise portal to wondrous adventures. Discover what many loyal fans who made Sovereign Stone a top d20 campaign already know: a rich world vibrating with novelty and excitement at every turn.

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, published by Wizards of the Coast, Inc. This product utilizes updated material from the v.3.5 revision.
