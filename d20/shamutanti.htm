Title: The Shamutanti Hills
Type: Adventure
For: 3rd level characters
Author: Jamie Wallis
Published: 2003
Publisher: Myriador
Format: 40-page book
It was a night that many people will never forget.
The night of the Black Moon.
The night that the Crown of Kings was stolen from the quiet and peaceful province known as Analand.
Your homeland.

You have been given the task of returning the Crown of Kings - stolen by Xamen Birdmen and now in the hands of the Archmage of Mampang.

This is the first part of your epic adventure, which will take you across the perilous Shamutanti Hills of Kakhabad. Will you survive the deadly Headhunters, Elvins and Hill Giants and make it safelt to Kharé - Cityport of Traps?
