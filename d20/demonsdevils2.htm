Demons and devils of legend walk the planes!

Awesome new role-playing experiences

  • Mankind's history provides us many legendary demons and devils of amazing power. Their historical abilities have been translated into d20 powers and abilities that will seriously challenge player characters.
  • Prestige classes for the damned and nearly damned. Many of the demons and devils have divine natures. These ultra powerful creatures naturally give some of their powers and skills to those who slavishly worship them.
  • These outsiders have brought with them some serious magic items. Weapons, bracelets, and rings are just a few of the new treasures you will find. Funny thing about these devices: when the outsider is banished back to its original plane, all these nice magic items just fall to the ground. Have you the courage to pick them up?
Visit www.fastforwardgames.com to get even more adventure, including 4 free new outsiders that won't be found anywhere else.

This Encyclopedia of Demons & Devils, Volume II is a d20 resource filled with new creatures, but there's a lot more here than meets the eye. The fun of this book is not only the legendary creatures but also their new magic items, spells, and prestige class characters, bringing new thrills and enjoyment to your fantasy role-playing game. These creatures are powerful new challenges to present to your player characters.

About the Authors:
A great many award-winning authors created the detailed descriptions in this volume: Anne Brown, Timothy Brown, Johnathan Cassie, Dale Donavan, B. D. Flory, Chris Hartford, Kurt Hausheer, Fred Jandt, Tim Kidwell, Kevin Melka, W. Jason Peck, George Strayton, Chris Trossen, and James M. Ward.
The powerful artwork comes from two primary talents, Jennifer Meyer and Tony Parker.
