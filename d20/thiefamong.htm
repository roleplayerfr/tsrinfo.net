Item Code: #SR1-2 Supplement
Title: A Thief Among Us
Type: Adventure
For: Single 1st-2nd level rogue
Author: John Merrill and Paul Taylor
Published: 2001
Publisher: Wyverns Claw Design
Format: 26-page PDF file
A Thief Among Us is a free addition to Thievery 101: The Periapt of Famidon, but is suitable for use in other ways. It details the NPCs attending a family reunion, the planned schedule of the evening, and a brief description of the rooms important to the adventure. It is intended for use with one 1st or 2nd level rogue of any alignment. Appropriate modifications should be made by the GM to challenge characters of a higher level. It is specifically tailored toward a PC that would rather use disguise and intrigue techniques to steal the Periapt. Further installments of the Thievery 101 line are available at www.wyvernsclaw.com.
