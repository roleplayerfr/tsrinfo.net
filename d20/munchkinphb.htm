D20... THE WAY IT SHOULD BE PLAYED!

Munchkin is the Origins Award-winning card game of dungeon delving. Now the great characters, monsters, and loot have flown off the cards... and right into your d20 game!

The Munchkin d20 Player's Handbook is the ultimate guide to roleplaying that is fast, dirty, and hellaciously fun! Fully compatible with other d20 games, Munchkin d20 will jazz up your sessions as your characters rocket up in power! This book includes munchkin classes, races, skills, feats, and spells designed to go through a dungeon like a rock through a window. (But DMs, don't worry... your book is coming soon...)

Watch for more Munchkin d20 books, coming soon from Steve Jackson Games!
