Of Places Most Foul

In these pages some of the darkest tales are told. Seven, horror-filled adventures ranging from 4th to 10th level await the players in your d20 campaign world.
These short, one to two session interludes will shock and surprise even the most experienced players.

You will also find four "Foul Locales" that are presented in frightening detail and ready to be plugged into your 3e game.

Plunge into the darkest of nightmares and the deepest pits of dispair. Oh, beware the evil that madmen do!

Face terrible demonic evil, foul undead, an inter-dimensional bar, evil and twisted men, dreadful curses, and much more.

Requires the use of Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
