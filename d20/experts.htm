Title: Experts
Type: Accessory
Author: Michael J. Varhola and Paul O. Knorr
Published: 2003
Publisher: Skirmisher Publishing
Format: 112-page perfect-bound book
One of the most intriguing and versatile classes described in the 3rd Edition Dungeons & Dragons Dungeon Master's Guide is the Expert. This comprehensive book thoroughly expands upon that brief description and is the ideal resource for anyone interested in designing highly skilled specialists of all sorts.

This book includes:

  • Nearly 30 fully detailed Expert types, including the Alchemist, Blacksmith, Courtesan, Merchant, Miner, Navigator, Physician, Sage, Sailor, and Weaponmaker.
  • Five broad metaclasses (Craftsman, Entertainers, Professionals, Sholars, and Tradesmen) that can be used as templates for quickly and easily creating all sorts of Experts.
  • Two new prestige classes, the Guildmaster and Militia Leader.
  • More than 100 new skills, including dozens of new Crafts and Professions and detailed rules for creation of everything from weapons and armor to buildings and ships.
  • Three new feats.
  • Guidelines for creating Experts with spellcasting abilities.
  • Information on creating Guilds and using them in the game.
  • 28 ready-to-use NPC Experts that can be used as-is or easily modified to suit any campaign or adventure.
  • 17 new magic items.
By Michael J. Varhola, Paul O. Knorr, and the Skirmisher Game Development Group. Foreword by Gary Gygax.
