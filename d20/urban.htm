The city never sleeps!

Welcome to the first in our new Foul Locales series titled Urban Blight.
Within these pages you will find 19 highly detailed plug-in locales that you can add to any d20 fantasy urban setting. These can be used as sources for ongoing adventures, useful places to visit, or the focus of urban intrigue.
Each Urban Locale has:
  • Detailed maps and location keys.
  • Fully fleshed out NPCs complete with motives and personalities.
  • An indepth story with adventure ideas and plot hooks.
Also in this book you will find new magical items, spells, feats, classes and more as you delve into deadly and foul locales. No game master should be without this great d20 urban resource.
