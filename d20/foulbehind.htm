Your players just wandered into a hamlet and you, the GM were not ready!
Lucky for you that you had Behind the Gates at your finger tips.
This book, the 3rd in the Foul Locales line is full of highly detailed, fully realized and creative plug-in locations for any fantasy d20 game.
Behind the Gates gives you plenty of unique locales for any small town, village, hamlet or just about any other small community.

Here is what you get in Behind the Gates:

  • 16 Ready-to-Use Locales for village/small town environment
  • Campaign "generic" to minimize necessary customizing for your campaign
  • Standalone product which does not require previous Foul Locales to use
  • Expanded to 136 pages
  • Adventure Plot Hooks and Rumors for every locale for easier integration into your campaign
  • Suitable locales for characters level 4 thru 20
  • High quality maps clearly showing every locale
  • Complete NPC stat blocks for every major character (66 stat blocks in all)
  • Sidebar and item indices for easily locating material
  • Mix of new and exisiting Open Game Content including 3 diseases, 1 domain, 6 feats, 4 herbs, 7 magic items, 1 martial art style, 5 monsters, 6 prestige classes, 6 spells, and 3 templates
Requires the use of Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, Published by Wizards of the Coast.
