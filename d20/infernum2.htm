Infernum: Book of the Tormentor is the setting and Games Master's guide to the Infernum Roleplaying Game. It includes:

History, giving the publicly known history of the Infernum - if not the publicly accepted history - as well as details on timekeeping, festivals and day-to-day life in the Pit.

Infernography is a Circle-by-Circle overview on the whole Pit and the regions beyond.

Factions & Powers gives a write-up of all the major factions in the Pit, such as the Nine Houses, the mortal tribes and many others.

Adversaries has monsters and Non-Player Characters to challenge the Players with.

Campaigning provides campaign ideas and Games Mastering advice tailored to the Infernum setting.
