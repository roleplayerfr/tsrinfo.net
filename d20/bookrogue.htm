Title: The Book of Roguish Luck
Type: Accessory
Author: Wolfgang Baur
Published: 2005
Publisher: Malhavoc Press
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
The silent footstep. The turn of a card. The glitter of a knife in the dark, and quiet kill. These are the tools of adventurers who live by their wits, deep in the shadows. But even skilled rogues adore and worship Lady Luck, who often holds the difference between success and failure.

The Book of Roguish Luck offers a new luck subsystem for all classes, plus the bandit, gutter mage, and shadowsworn core classes and three new prestige classes. In addition, it presents expanded guild rules, the shadow magic school of rogue spells, expanded sneak attack rules, new equipment, more than 30 feats, and almost 80 spells.

This book expands thieves' guilds to include complete stats, resources, and initiation rituals. The Book of Roguish Luck also shows you how to fence goods, gamble to win, pick locks, bribe officials, and - simply put - raise hell. It even includes guidelines for use with Monte Cook's Arcana Evolved.

Stealthier. Smarter. Tricksier. Luckier.

Malhavoc Press is game designer Monte Cook's d20 System imprint devoted to publishing unusual magic, monsters, and evocative game elements that go beyond traditional fantasy. Malhavoc Press products exhibit a mastery of the d20 System rules that only one of the 3rd Edition Dungeons & Dragons designers can offer you.
