Item Code: #TLG3002
Title: World Builder
Type: Accessory
Author: Gary Gygax & Dan Cross
Published: 2003
Publisher: Troll Lord Games
Format: 176-page perfect-bound hardback book
Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.

In this tome, the grandmaster of roleplaying presents an arsenal of definition, lists, tables, and charts invaluable to game designers and setting creators. The World Builder details trees and herbs, terrain features, and everyday facts, from government structure to tensile strength of rope, and includes an illustrated guide to armor and weapons. All of these setting details are supplemented by rules for use with the d20 system and the Lejendary Adventure system, including useful notes on magical gems and spell components.

From the mundane to the extraordinary, the depth of information within this second volume of Gary Gygax's Fantasy World series stands legion.
