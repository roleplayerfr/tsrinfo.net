Title: Evil in the Heart
Type: Adventure
For: Character levels 2-4
Author: Noel S. Maddow
Published: 2001
Publisher: Ubicorm IAC Game Company

Children missing, buildings burned, fear spreading. Could this be as simple as a humanoid incursion into settled territory? Or, Could it be something much more. Adventure and intrigue await as the heros must make daring rescues and delve deep into an underground lair of some unknown evil. Only the brave will venture and only the strong wil survive -- Evil in the Heart!
