BEHOLD THE ITEMS OF POWER!

Pocket Magica is your indispensable reference guide to the magic items of the d20 System. Pocket Magica includes armor and shields, cursed items, potions, rings, rods, staffs, wands, weapons, and wondrous items, arranged alphabetically within each section for easy reference. This tome contains all the magic items from the DMG, plus more from over a dozen of the best d20 sourcebooks, including Spells & Spellcraft, Bluffside: City on the Edge, Arms & Armor, Hammer & Helm: A Guidebook to Dwarves, and Freeport: The City of Adventure. All the magic items have been edited for clarity and revised with all known errata, making them the most up to date versions in print.

Two companion volumes of spells, Pocket Grimoire Arcane and Pocket Grimoire Divine, are also available. Whether you are a player or a game master, the books of the Pocket series are sure to find a permanent place on your gaming table.

Pocket Magica requires the Dungeons and Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast for use. Dungeons and Dragons and Wizards of the Coast are Registered Trademarks of Wizards of the Coast, and are used with Permission.
