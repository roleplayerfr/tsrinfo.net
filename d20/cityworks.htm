Item Code: #DD42
Title: Cityworks
Type: Accessory
Author: Mike Mearls
Published: 2003
Publisher: Fantasy Flight Games
Format: 176-page perfect-bound hardback book
Cityworks is the definitive d20 System resource for designing and running exciting adventures and campaign in fantasy cities.

  • New rules for characters adventuring in cities, including alternate core classes, prestige classes, feats, spells, and character design and development guidelines.
  • A detailed overview of fantasy cities, including their origins, histories, factions, governments, economies, and religions.
  • A complete and robust city design system.
  • Urban event templates, including the great fire, plague, siege, and revolt.
  • A complete and highly detailed random generation system for buildings, city blocks, and NPCs.

This book was originally solicited under the title Citycraft.
