Land of the Pharaohs

1570 BC. The Hyksos have been cast out of Egypt and the New Kingdom begun, but the Nile Empire is in danger. Twice, the evil god Set has murdered his brother Osiris, King of the Gods. Now the gods of Egypt are split into rival pantheons, waging war through their mortal Avatars.

NILE EMPIRE: WAR IN HELIOPOLIS is a d20 system sourcebook on the New Kingdom of Egypt, detailing the gods, weapons, character classes, feats and skills needed to role-play in the Valley of the Kings. A bevy of new monsters appear, plus the outline for a 10-adventure campaign set amidst the war of the gods. Are you ready for jackal-headed Anubis to lay your deeds alongside the Feather of Truth, or will your soul be condemned to feed the Destroyer?
