Over One Thousand Feats!

Ultimate Feats is the second Supplementary Rulebook of the Ultimate Series, compiling some of the best feats available to characters anywhere. Designed to be seamlessly slotted into any fantasy-based, or more modern, d20 games system, these sourcebooks offer the best of the best that have appeared in terms of rules and options for both fantasy and futuristic d20 games, as well as new material that can be easily included in any campaign. Players and Games Masters will find a wealth of options to enrich their characters and antagonists.

With a multitude of feats that cover everything from exotic weapon training, to the creation of diverse items, to calling upon the powers of the divine and much, much more, Ultimate Feats is a comprehensive and invaluable aid to any d20 Player or Games Master.
