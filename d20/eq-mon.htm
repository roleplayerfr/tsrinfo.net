It's Your World Now!
Discover the dangers that threaten Norrath, the world of EverQuest. This collection of EverQuest's monsters provides new information about the world of Norrath through the backgrounds of its monsters, shows the strengths and weaknesses of Norrath's most challenging enemies, and provides all pf the statistics you need to place these foes into your own EverQuest story.

100% compatible with 3rd edition fantasy role-playing rules
To test heroes of all levels, this illustrated volume introduces a host of monsters from the lowly fire beetle to Cazic-Thule, the God of Fear.
