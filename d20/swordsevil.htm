Item Code: #FAF2013
Title: Swords of Evil
Type: Accessory
Published: 2002
Publisher: Fast Forward Entertainment
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
Magical Swords, Powerful Villains:

When the two meet, all hell breaks loose!

Swords of Evil presents 22 of the deadliest artifacts ever created, each held by a villain powerful enough in his own right - though whether they control their swords or their swords control them is entirely a different matter! Each weapon has a secret of its own and is carefully guarded by a force of villain that could - and have - toppled entire kingdoms!

  • Each of these incredibly powerful, and exceptionally vile, weapons is described in detail, with an emphasis on its motivations and magical powers.
  • No sword could be complete without a warrior to wield it, including even these potent relics. Those who possess these malevolent blades are themselves powerful and reviled scourges, and are described in detail right along with their blades.
Every villain needs a lair, and these formidable individuals are no exception! When they are not conquering and spreading their malevolence, they are choosing their next targets here. The inner sanctums of each of these cruel foes is described in just as much detail, giving Dungeon Masters and Players alike a full gaming experience!

Evil stalks the land in the form of incredibly powerful magical blades. These weapons each have their own deadly agendas and command nearly unstoppable powers. The only force standing in the way of ultimate destruction is you. Are you ready to take up the challenge and test your metal against the deadliest of evil blades?

Fast Forward Entertainment assembled an incredibly talented group of authors, including winners of the pretigious Origins Award, to bring you this unique accessory. Together, Johnathan Cassie, Kurt Hausheer, Tim Kidwell, Kevin Melka, Stephen D. Sullivan and James M. Ward have crafted a book that will bring Dugeon Masters and Players alike hundreds of hours of enjoyment!
