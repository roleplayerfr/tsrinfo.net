FEARED WARRIORS OF THE ARCANE

Cold. Cunning. Masters of sword and spell. Such words describe the serpentfolk called asaatthi, yet still little is known of their ancient history, their rich culture, their once-influential role in the world... and their plans for the future.

This second volume in the Vigil Watch series for the Scarred Lands explores the mysterious asaath race. Also discover tips for using these serpentfolk in any d20 campaign, from details on the creatures' culture and lairs to unique magic and prestige classes.

REQUIRES THE USE OF THE DUNGEONS & DRAGONS PLAYER'S HANDBOOK, VERSION 3.5, PUBLISHED BY WIZARDS OF THE COAST.
