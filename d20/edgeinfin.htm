AT THE EDGE OF INFINITY,
WHERE DWELL THE GODS

Even the vast Scarred Lands are tiny compared to the scope of the Great Beyond. From the horrendous wastes of the Apocalypse Abyss to the beatific glories of the Adamantine Heavens, these planar realms are places of mystery and wonder - not in the least because of the role they played in the gods' empowerment and the titans' downfall.
Edge of Infinity: The Scarred Planes is a sourcebook detailing the dimensional planes which enfold and enwrap the Scarred Lands, from the homes of the gods to the stellar planes to the planes of the elements.

REQUIRES THE USE OF THE DUNGEONS & DRAGONS PLAYER'S HANDBOOK, PUBLISHED BY WIZARDS OF THE COAST. THIS PRODUCT UTILIZES UPDATED MATERIAL FROM THE V.3.5 REVISION.
