BEWARE THE MINIONS OF THE SPIDER QUEEN!

Descend into the depths and learn the secrets of the wickedest race in the Underdark, the drow. Third in Green Ronin's Races of Renown series, Plot & Poison is a rules toolkit for players and gamemasters alike. Dragon Magazine editor Matthew Sernett delves deep into the heart of the drow, providing a book packed with new options, including:

  • Complete rules for player character drow, and four new drow subraces.
  • 16 new prestige classes, including the Darksight Slayer and the Vile Tormentor.
  • Over 75 new feats.
  • The Spider Queen and Her Eight Claws: a complete drow pantheon.
  • A treasury of new equipment and magic items, and the introduction of verminous items.
  • Rules for over 50 poisons, including alchemical poisons.
  • Over 20 new domains and scores of new magic spells.
Plot & Poison is your indispensable guide to elves you love to hate.

WHAT IS OGL INTERLINK?

Green Ronin Publishing and Paradigm Concepts have teamed up to create OGL Interlink, a series of related sourcebooks for the d20 System. Initially, these books will come from Paradigm's Races of Legend series and Green Ronin's Races of Renown series. If you enjoy Plot & Poison, you may want to check out Unveiled Masters: The Essential Guide to Mind Flayers from Paradigm. Stop by out website for more information.

Plot & Poison requires the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast for use. Dungeons and Dragons and Wizards of the Coast are Registered Trademarks of Wizards of the Coast, and are used with Permission.
