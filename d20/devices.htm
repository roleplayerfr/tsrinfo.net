Title: Devilish Devices

Published: 2003

A trove of powerful and dark items at your fingertips!

Devilish Devices gives you 50 new and unique magical items to add to your fantasy campaigns. Whether you are looking for a powerful enemy to defeat or for an artifact to quest for, this book provides everything you need for a memorable adventure but your imaginations! Within these pages are the background details on each of these incredibly powerful relics, including who possesses it now and who is already after it, all presented in a storyteller format that makes it ideal for easy inclusion in any campaign. Are you prepared to write the next chapter in the tales of these artifacts?

Can You Characters Handle It?

Every hero has his arch-nemesis and every world has its uber-villain. These are no normal villains, for each possesses one of the many evil devices described in this tome. The dark forces they command are staggering, making them threats to even the gods themselves. Do your Player Characters have what it takes to put an end to their reign of terror? Do they have the guts to make the ultimate sacrifice, if that is what it takes?

Devilish Devices includes:

  • 50 devilish devices, from crystal balls to torture implements, flagons to earrings. All of these are fully compatible with the d20 System, ready to drop into any fantasy campaign.
  • Every device is presented in both a story and in d20 statistics. The story portion of each entry recounts the item's history and the source of its power. The statistics section delivers each item's game stats plus those of all the characters, monsters or obstacles interwoven into its story.
  • Additionally, six new Prestige Classes, each related in some way to one of the items detailed within, are included, giving both game masters and players alike new classes of characters to interact with.
  • Wonderful illustrations of every single item. Each rendering is completely accurate and true to the magical items.

These are wondrous devices in every sense of the word!
