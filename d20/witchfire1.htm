Item Code: #WF001
Title: The Longest Night
Type: Adventure
For: 1st - 3rd level characters
Author: Mark Staroscik
Published: 2001
Publisher: Privateer Press
Format: 64-page perfect-bound book
She was only a child when she saw her mother executed for the crime of witchcraft.

She only wants to have her mother back. Is that so wrong?

But sometimes, if you want to get something done, you have to knock down a city with a horde of undead.

Enter the IRON KINGDOMS in this first book of a landmark new campaign environment! Explore a world where magic and technology go hand in hand, where terror stalks the night in many forms and ancient artifacts guide the destinies of men!

Book one of the Witchfire Trilogy provides the perfect entry point to the IRON KINGDOMS. Half adventure, half source material, this book will provide you with weeks of campaign material as well as new monsters, locales, magical creations, and intriguing characters to use for years to come.

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.

An adventure for characters of 1st-3rd level.
