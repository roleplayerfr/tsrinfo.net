WAVES OF SCARLET

Shackled in a trench deep in the ocean floor lies the mighty titan Kadum, the Father of Monsters, his chest ever bleeding from the hole where his heart was plucked out. The Mountainshaker's endless blood has transformed the waves into a vast crimson sea that changes everything it touches - men and beasts alike.
This sourcebook contains information for game play in and around the Blood Sea, from its creation to those who owe their existence to it. Venture from the surface of the red waves to the very sanguine depths of the crimson expanse, and even to unique lands such as the Heartsblood Archipelago.

REQUIRES THE USE OF THE DUNGEONS & DRAGONS PLAYER'S HANDBOOK, PUBLISHED BY WIZARDS OF THE COAST. THIS PRODUCT UTILIZES UPDATED MATERIAL FROM THE V.3.5 REVISION.

Sword and Sorcery books are published under the Open Game License and are 100% compatible with revised 3rd Edition rules.
