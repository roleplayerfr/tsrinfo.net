GOD * KING * COUNTRY

For thousands of years we have fought. If not with the orcs and goblins and ogres and miscreants so foul it burns your eyes just to say their names, we have warred within. None know more the ways of warfare and combat than our people. We have suffered under the banne of oppression or fought under the flags of prosperity, but always we have fought to keep ourselves safe.

I have sworn an oath to die protecting my king and I pledge my sword to forever serve him. I pledge to rid this place of all beasts foul and contemptable. May the people of Westbrook never know fear again, so long as we ride in valor and nobility to challenge the unkempt creatures of the earth.

For the people!

-General Anyk'dar, High Commander of Westbrook and Last Defender of the Wind
