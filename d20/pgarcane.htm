MAGIC AT YOUR FINGERTIPS!

The Pocket Grimoires are your indispensable reference guides to the spells of the d20 System. Pocket Grimoire Aracane includes spells for Assassins, Bards, Sorcerers, and Wizards, arranged alphabetically for easy reference. This tome contains all the arcane spells from the PH, plus spells from over a dozen of the best d20 sourcebooks, including the Book of Eldritch Might, Traps and Treachery, Codex Arcanis, The Tide of Years, and Freeport: The City of Adventure. All the spells have been edited for clarity and revised with all known errata, making them the most up to date versions in print.

A companion volume, Pocket Grimoire Divine, is also available for those who heed the call of the gods. Whether you are a player of a game master, the Pocket Grimoires are sure to find a permanent place on your gaming table.
