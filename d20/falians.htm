The Tribe of Shadows

Unlike the other Earth Goddess Tribes, the folk of the Tribe of Shadows place little importance on directness as a virtue. They will happily sneak up behind an enemy and strangle him, not thinking it the least bit dishonourable. Before army army brings them to battle, they may find their general assassinated and warriors out of action through a does of hallucinogenic fungi in the mead cauldron. Once in combat they will not shirk from stabbing in the back if the oppurtunity arises, but their courage on the open battlefield is no less than that of any other Earth Goddess tribe and they will proudly fight to the death in honour duels or battles alike.
