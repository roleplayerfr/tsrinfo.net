MUNCHKIN.
IT'S NOT JUST FOR PLAYERS ANY MORE.

What can a Munchkin GM do?
Whatever he wants. Player annoys you? Kill him. Or change his sex. Or turn him into a Canada goose. Go all the way and turn him into a dead sex-changed Canada goose. No one can stop you. When you're the GM, the rules are only suggestions - and mild ones at that.
So the Munchkin Master's Guide is 48 pages of evil suggestions: magic items, powerful NPC classes, terrifying tables, and a loaded 100-sided die's worth of dirty tricks.

Remember - It's good to be the GM.

Requires the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
