Title: Munchkin Monster Manual
Type: Accessory
Author: John W. Mangrum
Published: 2003
Publisher: Steve Jackson Games
Format: 48-page perfect-bound hardback book
It's all about the thrill of combat... meeting worthy foes and murdering them by surprise... gleefully slaughtering their low-level minions... defiling the corpses... and, of course, TAKING THEIR STUFF.
The Munchkin Monster Manual contains dozens of foes for you and your comrades to slay, from the dreaded Gazebo to the despicable Potted Plant! And yes, of course there are stats for Plutonium Dragons. Of all ages...

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
