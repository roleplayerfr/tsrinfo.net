Mystical Weapons of Legend and Legacy...

Epic tales are filled with mythic figures and the mighty weapons they carry ever at their sides. Whether in the form of a warrior's blade or wizard's cudgel, these legendary items contain eldritch powers unleashed by the will and might of those destined to wield them.

Artifacts of the Ages: Swords and Staves introduces magic weapons, staves, and rods that grow in power as wielders advance in level, allowing you to put artifact-caliber arms and tools in the hands of characters while maintaining balanced game play throughout a heroic campaign.

Artifacts of the Ages: Swords and Staves includes:

  • Fifteen unique magic weapons, including twelve swords, such as the Sword of Kings and Draakhoorn, the Dragon's Doom
  • Fourteen magical staves and rods, such as the Staff of Doors and Bramblethorn, the Arm of the Woods
  • Four new prestige classes that allow characters to access special abilities of legendary weapons: the battle scion, spell scion, faith scion, and swift scion
  • Guidelines for how to incorporate these mighty items into your own campaign
Artifacts of the Ages: Swords and Staves is a printed compilation of the first two titles in an innovative series of sourcebooks examining new ways to treat magic items. Originally released in PDF-format, the material in this book has been updated to incorporate player feedback and to be fully compatible with the latest d20 System core rulebooks.
