Welcome to the second edition of our sourcebook. Incredibly, it is a free product. 0one Roleplaying Games is still growing and The Legend of the Steel General stimulated the imagination of many gamers.
In this sourcebook, we have revised, corrected and added many new things. Some are found in The Legend of the Steel General, some are brand-new for you to use in our forthcoming adventure: The Twenty Sides of the Evil, the second long-awaited chaper of The Seven Avengers Saga. You will find new feats, skills and spells, several races, two prestige classes, a new class and an explanation of how magic works. Furthermore, the regions of the western kingdoms are described in more detail. This helps you start and continue the Seven Avengers Saga.
Enjoy this sourcebook and welcome to the world of The Seven Avengers.
