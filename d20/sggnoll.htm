Title: The Slayer's Guide to Gnolls
Type: Accessory
Published: 2001
Publisher: Mongoose Publishing
Format: 32-page book
ADVANCED MENU AVOIDANCE

The second supplement in the acclaimed series from Mongoose Publishing, The Slayer's Guide to Gnolls focuses on this bloodthirsty race, from their brutal and savage society to their lethal tactics of ambush.

Ravenous packs of gnolls are the bane of many adventurers. Inhuman in the extreme, this vicious race delights in acts of cruelty and brutality against the weak, the defenceless and the innocent. This book contains everything you need to hunt down and annihilate these evil creatures.

FOR GAMES MASTERS AND PLAYERS ALIKE
Requires the use of the Dungeons and Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
