Title: Ultimate Equipment Guide
Type: Accessory
Author: Matthew Sprange
Published: 2003
Publisher: Mongoose Publishing
Format: 256-page perfect-bound book
What would a fighter be without his armour, a wizard without his spellbook or a rogue without his vast array of strange bits of metal that he insists are absolutely not lockpicks?

The Ultimate Equipment Guide is far more than a simple list of mundane items, instead it delves into the heart of the d20 system, bringing a vast array of diverse items from the mundane to the fantastical. This massive tome brings together the best equipment from a wide range of Open Game Content sources, together with a host of new items for your characters to spend their hard-won gold on.

Your equipment can often make the difference between life and death for your characters, with the Ultimate Equipment Guide your chances of survival just got a lot better.
