Title: Gladiator: Sands of Death
Type: Accessory
Author: Matthew Sprange
Published: 2001
Publisher: Mongoose Publishing
Format: 80-page perfect-bound book
Forty thousand spectators watch each footstep, every movement of your sword as you close upon your opponent. The life of a gladiator is one of servitude and slavery but with every victory comes the howl of the mob, driving you ever more to succeed. You are one of the greatest combatants every to have walked onto the sands of death and your name is known across the land. Fame, fortune and eventual freedom are yours for the taking if you can only survive as a gladiator.

Arenas & Campaigns - integrate gladitorial combat into your gaming world. Mighty arenas dominate their cities and entertain the masses as nobles subtly manipulate matches in order to retain control of the population.

Gladitorial Combat - Become a gladiator and learn the tricks of the trade. New feats and prestige classes await alongside a multitude of different matches and low-down tactics to grant both victory and the love of the crowd.

Sands of Death - A complete game based on the D20 system. Each player takes the part of a stable master, grooming his gladiators to make a fortune within the arena.

Bringing the spectacle to the masses
