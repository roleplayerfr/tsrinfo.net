Item Code: #SGL1001
Title: Brotherhood of Prophecy
Type: Adventure
For: 1st-3rd level characters
Author: Chuck McGrew
Published: 2001
Publisher: Scarab Games
Format: 32-page book
A year ago, one of the trade caravans into the Northlands failed to return. No one thought much of it at the time - trade with the Northlands has always been hazardous. The next two also failed to return. Now trade with the Northlands has ceased.

Six months ago, the Brotherhood of Prophecy used its signal mirror to shine a bright distress call. Immediately border troops were sent deep into Ardenwood to assist the monks. Two days later, the distress signals ceased. The guards never returned.

Fearing that the orc and goblin hordes had overtaken the monastery, the Lord ordered his best scouts and soldiers to investigate. That was three months ago. They were never seen again. Additional troops cannot be sent without leaving Tageth unprotected. Lord Grimlee has offered a reward of 1,000 gold pieces to anyone who can reach the monastery and return with news of what has happened.
