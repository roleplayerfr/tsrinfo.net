THE SECRETS OF THE CRAFT ARE YOURS!

The witch is a "naturalist" spellcaster who casts arcane spells using time-honored hearth wisdom rather than the arcane formulae and elaborate theories of wizardry. The Witch's Handbook, like other books in the Master Class series, presents a brand-new core class and all the information you need to use this class in your campaign. Within these pages you'll find:

  • 5 prestige classes, such as the Infernal Witch and Witch Hag.
  • 15 new feats, including Swarm Shape and Lunar Magic.
  • Over a dozen new spells and new uses for old spells like Bestow Curse.
  • Rules for ritual and circle magic, based on those in Sword & Sorcery Studios' Relics and Rituals sourcebook.
  • 30 new potions, and an assortment of other magic items, both cursed and wondrous.
  • Expanded rules for the Heal and Profession (Herbalism) skills, an assortment of herbal remedies and toxins, and suggestions for herbal spell components.
  • Full stats for a variety of Witch NPCs.
  • A Witch Addendum to Green Ronin's popular Character Record Folio.
  • Beautiful art from Drew Baker, Storm Cook, and Stephanie Pui-mun Law!
Whether you're a player looking for a different kind of magic user or a gamemaster looking to add something new to your campaign, The Witch's Handbook has everything you ned to bring The Craft to your d20 game!
