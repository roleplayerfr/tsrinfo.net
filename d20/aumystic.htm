Title: Unearthed Arcana: Mystic Secrets: The Lore of Word and Rune
Type: Accessory
Author: Mike Mearls
Published: 2004
Publisher: Malhavoc Press
Format: 72-page perfect-bound book
Magic means power in the world of the Diamond Throne. Witches, runethanes, and magisters can dictate the course of history with the spells and runes they wield. They jealously guard their secrets - but this book unveils them all for use in your campaign.

This sourcebook expands the magical options available to all characters in Monte Cook's Arcana Unearthed. Original ceremonies allow characters to forge mystic bonds with allies and even weapons. Additional rules for runes expand their abilities while revealing the secrets behind their true nature. New spells and magic items enhance any character's abilities. And unique mystic sites provide intriguing new vistas for magical experiments and adventure.

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Third Edition Core Books, published by Wizards of the Coast. Requires the use of Monte Cook's Arcana Unearthed, published by Malhavoc Press.
