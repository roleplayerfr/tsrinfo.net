A Fate Worse Than Death

Something has awakened in the Mistwood Mines. An old evil stretches its tentacles across the Moon Fog Hills and once again the hearty folk below have a reason to live in terror. Are you brave enough to discover the secrets of the mines? Can you withstand the Obelisks of Chaos?

A Slithering Chaos at the Core

Designed for 4-6 characters of Levels 5-8, Aberrations features challenging wilderness encounters, twisting caverns of dread, and an extensively detailed manor house full of secret passages, devious traps, and gruesome foes to test your party at every twist and turn. Included are several new monsters for use in any D20 System campaign. Come and be a guest of the Mathen Estate, just don't plan on staying too long.
