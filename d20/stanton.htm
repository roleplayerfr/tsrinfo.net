Title: St. Anton's Fire
Type: Adventure
For: Levels 5-7
Author: Caroline Parker
Published: 2002
Publisher: Troll Lord Games
Format: 48-page book
Nothing stirs in St. Anton. Through alleys and streets, along castle walls, the town lies in a dreamlike lull. Moonlight shines upon cobblestone paths winding lazily up and down small hills. Sagging balconies and arching trees bow idly over the quiet streets.

Evil is Afoot

The Baron has descended into insanity, an eldritch creature stalks the streets, and clouds of despair gather over the townspeople. St. Anton needs brave heroes to unravel its mystery, and restore her fire.
