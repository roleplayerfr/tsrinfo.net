Title: Sovereign Stone: Marauders of the Wolf: The Dwarves
Type: Accessory
Author: James M. Ward & Michael Lichucki
Published: 2003
Publisher: Sovereign Press
Format: 144-page perfect-bound book
The dwarves of Loerem are a unique race that live under the open skies, practice the magic of Fire and venerate the sacred Wolf. This volume details the culture, lands, and personalities of the great clans and the magnificent cities of the Unhorsed.

Marauders of the Wolf: The Dwarves provides a wealth of detail for any player of a dwarven character or a referee designing a campaign featuring dwarves and their lands. Included are new feats, spells, magical items, and rules for mounted combat. Brand new prestige classes provide a host of new options for dwarven characters, from the scholarly Order of the Intelligent to the deadly Deathshooters.
