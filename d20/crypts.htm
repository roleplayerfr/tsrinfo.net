Looking For Something Different?

Tired of another evening of the same-old, same-old? Are your players? Enchanted Locations gives you something different - quests! What better way to energize your games?

No matter what kind of gaming group you've got, there's something within these pages for you. Need some more powerful artifacts to put into your players' hands... or their enemies' hands? You've got it in here. Add to that a quest to find and return that item, and you've got yourself the makings for an unfogettable adventure. Just add characters and stir!

Worried About "Fitting In?"

Hate trying to fit other products into your own campaign? Worried about upsetting the delicate balance of your world... or ours? Don't worry! These Enchanted Locations can be fit into any campaign or world without a problem! They are detailed enough to build a simple evening encounter from and open enough to create an entire campaign around within your own adventure world.

What You'll Find Inside:

  • Rules on how to create and assign important quests to your characters.
  • 28 new artifacts, each belonging to one of seven sets of incredible power.
  • 35 different maps detailing the crypts, lairs and tombs where these artifacts can be found.
  • Details on the denizens of these crypts, lairs and tombs.
  • Guidelines on creating other treasure and rewards for your characters.
Visit www.fastforwardgames.com to learn more about this and other upcoming products.

Enchanted Locations: Crypts and Tombs is a d20 System campaign sourcebook that brings a little bit of everything into a single source, perfect for game masters looking to add a little bit of spice to their games. From new magical items to dangerous locales, this book has everything you need to build adventures for years to come!

About the Author:

James M. Ward has been designing incredible role-playing products for decades! Creator of the Greyhawk and Metamorphosis Alpha lines, Jim brings his own award-winning brand of adventure within these pages. Beware! This book isn't for the faint-of-heart PC!

Originally solicited under the title Enchanted Locations: Graveyards & Crypts.
