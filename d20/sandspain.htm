Item Code: #MYG7001
Title: Interludes: Sands of Pain
Type: Adventure
For: 4 characters, 4th level
Author: Jeffrey Quinn
Published: 2002
Publisher: Mystic Eye Games / Thunderhead Games
Format: 64-page perfect-bound book
The second adventure sourcebook in the Interludes series brings the characters from Bluffside south to the Dragon Sands. This adventure features a mix of horror and romance, done Arabic style. Sands of Pain can be used as a standalone adventure in any game world, or to enrich the Bluffside Campaign Setting. Web enhancements will be available in a special password protected area, free for your downloading pleasure.

Love's lament is perhaps the loudest. When a noble bard falls in love with a desert flower from the Dragon Sands, he will move mountains to get her back from her evil uncle. But there is more to this simple romance than first appears. Will the passion of these star-crossed lovers be forever extinguished, or will our heroes journey into the depths of the Sands of Pain?

Interludes: Sands of Pain includes:

  • An adventure for 4 player characters of 4th level
  • New creatures, magic items, equipment and a prestige class
  • new places of interest (POIs) in Bluffside and Kirkwood, the new town of Hotempa, and dozens of new, fully-detailed NPCs
  • Theme Boxes to adjust the adventure to suit almost any party
