Item Code: #SKP0403
Title: Nuisances
Type: Accessory
Author: Michael J. Varhola and Sharon Daughtery
Published: 2004
Publisher: Skirmisher Publishing
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
NUISANCES HAS MORE and is unlike any other fantasy RPG sourcebook! It is a tribute to the early years of fantasy roleplaying games, a parody of the current state of the genre, a manual for keeping cocky and annoying players off balance, and a poignant philosophical expression of the post-modern, 21st century worldview.

Highlights of this wonderful book include:

  • Hundreds and hundreds of aggravating and awful things that can happen to players!
  • Original art by about a half dozen fantasy artists!
  • Introduction of the Defect character attribute - and fewer than 100 Defects!
  • Bunches of unsavory Feats!
  • Several Archetypical Nuisance Chatacters, including Krust, Joebob, and Amber!
  • Cursed and off-color magic items!
  • Scores of defective items!
  • Many, many random d100, d12 and other Random Tables!
  • Rules that address the special role of female characters in the game in a sensitive and realistic way - including Feats like Nice Body and Defects like Bitch, Hot Flashes, and Throws Like a Girl!
  • Some of the most annoying monsters ever spawned, including the Decapussywhipper, the Dire Dwarf, and the world-famous Jester Dragon!
  • Unfortunate NPC personality traits!
  • An open game license!
  • More exclamation points than any other similar product!
