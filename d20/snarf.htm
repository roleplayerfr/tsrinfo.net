A Comic Quest for Wealth, Power, and all that Other Good Stuff...

written by Jamie Chambers & Larry Elmore

Looking to add some humor to your current 'd20' campaign? Or need to get reacquainted with the misadventures of your favorite Zeetva? This 128-page book is packed full of 'd20' material; including a brand-new character class, prestige classes, feats, skills, spells, races, monsters, magical items, and DM advice, including comical adventure hooks.

We created this sourcebook with three objectives in mind...

  • To give DMs great advice on how to add a comical feel to their games
  • To provide a detailed plot summary of the SnarfQuest stories - a summary that's as entertaining for new readers as it is insightful to longtime fans of the graphic novel.
  • To furnish insider information about SnarfQuest, from the creator, Larry Elmore!
It doesn't matter if you are a DM, a player, or just someone searching for an interesting new world to adventure in... this book has just what you are looking for!

All hail the return of Snarf!
