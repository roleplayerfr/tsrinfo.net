Item Code: #APL909
Title: Black Flags: Piracy in the Caribbean
Type: Adventure
Published: 2002
Publisher: Avalanche Press LTD
Format: 64-page perfect-bound book
Sail the REAL Seven Seas!

Take your d20 System campaign to a new century of adventuring and discover. This is the essential guidebook to role-play 18th Century pirates and privateers in the Caribbean. This book is loaded with historical information on life at sea, the political division of the New World and colorful characters like Mary Read and Anne Bonny who made pirates into legends. It includes five new characters classes, three character class templates and four new prestige classes. There's also a host of new feats and skills, rules for ship-to-ship combat, a special section on adding some panache to your play, and a re-evaluation of firearms combat reflective of the period.
