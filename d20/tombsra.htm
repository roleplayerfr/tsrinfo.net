The Fabled Valley of the Kings...

...has stood all but untouched for millennia. Few of those that have ever been within its confines have returned alive - and none have returned unchanged. It is a place of honor where the most powerful rulers and intellectual leaders of the greatest civilizations of the distant past are interred. But those civilizations are dead, as are any who would venerate these dark crypts. Now, the valley is ruled by those left behind to guard it from thieves and grave robbers.

Treasure and Danger Abound!

Only the greatest and most respected leaders are interred in the Valley of the Kings. Befitting their station, they were surrounded with only the best and most valuable of gifts to see them off into the afterlife. But just as their people left behind great riches, so too did they leave behind powerful guardians to protect the tombs of their heroes from those who would defile and plunder them. The Valley of the Kings promises riches beyond the dreams of avarice, but any looking to claim that prize must first face the traps and guardians left behind - and they defend their wealth jealously.

The Secrets of Ages Past!

This isn't just a realm for t he dead. The valley is something far more than just a place to bury the dead. Dark dark forces operate within the Valley of the Kings, searching for an escape from an everlasting prison, while other powerful brings watch carefully over the valley to ensure that nothing escapes its confines. Or no one. Do your characters have the courage to face the deadly challenges of this fabled land?

Treasure Quests: Tombs of Ra is a d20 System campaign accesory detailing the Valley of Kings, an ancient burial ground for kings, prophets and wise men from all races and cultures. Contained within this book are details on more than thirty different tombs, including the creatures and traps guarding them, as well as rules on how to design the rest of the scores of tombs that populate the valley. These tombs represent a wide range of challenges for Player Character groups and are suitable for use as either a linked campaign or individual adventures in any fantasy campaign setting.
