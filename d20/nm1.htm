Item Code: (NM1)
Title: The Upside Down Tower
Type: Adventure
For: Characters level 1-3
Author: Noel S. Maddow
Published: 2001
Publisher: Ubicorm IAC Game Company
Format: 24-page book
Does Great treasures await those brave enough to seek it? Or does doom wait for the foolish enough to try? Lost centuries ago, The Upside Down Tower has been one man's lifelong work but now, he might not live to see his dreams come to fruition. Will the heroes manage to recover some long lost artifact and hoard of treasure that is surely there or will they meet their ultimate fate in a vain attempt to complete a fool's errand? The only way to find out is to breach -- The Upside Down Tower.
