Villains for All Heroes...

Heroes come in all sizes, shapes and, most importantly, all levels of experience. Villains who could kill a group of 2nd-level PCs wouldn't even last ten seconds against a party of 12th-level adventurers. Included in this book are dozens of different villains of varying levels of challenge, giving you a wide range of bad guys to throw at your heroes. From their initial steps out into the world and throughout the course of their adventures, your players won't know what hit them.

...and for All Occasions

In the dozens of villians contained within these pages, you'll find bad guys that your PCs can encounter in almost any region and circumstance. But these are far more than just villains to be killed in one sitting. These are individuals that your PCs can come across time and time again during the course of their adventures. Perhaps one will hire those intrepid adventurers of yours to retrieve a stolen artifact only to turn on them years later. Or maybe one will just make it his life's goal to rid the world of your PCs...

Make Life Easy on Yourself

The Encyclopedia of Villains is here to aid you in your Game Mastering duties. You don't always have the time to come up with a full adventure. The army of villains contained in this book will give you countless different adventure nuggets: just add PCs and go!

Evil haunts the land in many different forms, not all of which are readily identifiable. When a tribe of bugbears sets up camp in a forest, it's easy to find them and end their reign of terror, but few villains are so obvious. Most operate in secrecy, working through minions and allies to achieve their goals unnoticed. The Encyclopedia of Villains is a d20 System sourcebook that provides Game Masters a host of cunning villains, ready to use and easily dropped into any fantasy setting.
