Extensively researched and faithfully rendered, Legends of Excalibur provides all the mechanics for adventuring in the world of Athurian Legend. This extensive tome presents players with over twenty new Arthurian classes, an alternative magic system, and a flavorful system for fate and destiny. For game masters, Legends of Excalibur offers a detailed campaign guide, including NPC statistics, adventure locations, quests, campaign models, color maps, and other tools.
