Title: The Crucible of Freya: Supplemental Adventure Ideas and Encounter Areas
Type: Adventure
Author: Clark Peterson and Bill Webb
Published: 2001
Publisher: Necromancer Games
Format: 23-page PDF file
Your players have defeated the orcs in the Ruined Keep and returned to Fairhill as heroes. Now they want more! This free supplement provides further adventures set in and around Fairhill. It is an extension of the Necromancer Games module The Crucible of Freya and requires that module for use.
