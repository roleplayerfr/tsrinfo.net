Title: Arcana Evolved: Spell Treasury
Type: Accessory
Author: Monte Cook and Jeffery A. Dobberpuhl
Published: 2006
Publisher: Malhavoc Press
Format: 240-page perfect-bound hardback book
Magic may be the most fascinating of all the interesting aspects of Monte Cook's Arcana Evolved. This sourcebook expands that system with more than 500 spells - magic converted from the System Reference Document, The Complete Book of Eldritch Might, and other sources, as well as all-new spells. Every spell comes complete and ready to use with the flexible Arcana Evolved magic system, including heightened and diminished effects, magic item creation modifiers, and more.

This Spell Treasury more than triples the repetoire of Arcana Evolved spellcasters, from magisters and witches to runthanes, greenbonds, and mage blades. Also includes sidebars on the spells' uses and introduces the new runic and soul descriptors. Suitable for characters of all levels.
