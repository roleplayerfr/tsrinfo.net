LIFE * DEATH * IN BETWEEN

The plague of all living things is death, the beauty of horror inextricably linked through poetry and art, knowledge and faith. It cannot be stopped. It calls each of us with impunity. But death, by all, is still feared. Rumors of a hellish afterlife shake the foundation of every religion. Often, the spiritual core is challenged by one's own dependence on the flesh. A fate worse than hell, a living damnation awaits those who challenge the rites of the gods.

Yet still some seek eternity, delving into the arcane lore of the darkest secrets. The gates between life and unlife sunder under the blows of faithless men who forsake their souls at the expense of the living.

Damn them all.

 -Father Einik of the Order of Weeping and Wailing

THIS IS A GUIDE TO IMMORTALITY

This sourcebook contains everything a GM or player needs for campaigns and adventures involving undeath. All of the torments of hell are bound within these pages. Undead and undead hunter prestige classes, magic items and artifacts for life and unlife, and details on the rituals of mummification and lichdom are detailed within.

The text within this tome is forbidden, its secrets damnable.
The records of undeath cost more than any can afford.
