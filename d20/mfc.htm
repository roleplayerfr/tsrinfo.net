Green is Beautiful!

They may not all be physically green, but deep down inside they all want the same thing - to smash, break and destroy everything in sight. Well, you're finally going get your chance to play one of them! You can have your choice of playing a bugbear, derro, DragonGoblin, drow, duergar, gnoll, half-orc, hobgoblin, kobold, ogre, an orc or one of two types of goblins or trolls. And if they aren't your cup of tea, you've got your choice of five good races as well!

Time for Something Completely Different...

Did you ever want to try something different? Are you sick of hunting down yet another band of marauding goblins? Well, it's time to turn the tables! You and your group can now take on the roles of those goblins! You know how to kill them, but do you have what it takes to keep them alive, or even to hunt down those evil humans and elves and dwarves that keep invading your homelands and cutting down your brethren?

Your Complete Monster Fighter's Resource!

Whether you're running a humanoid from a primitive culture, an incredibly powerful (but dumb-as-rocks) ogre or an evil drow who lives deep below the surface, this book has something for you. Inside are four new base character classes and a dozen more new prestige classes, each designed for the monstrous fighter. There are new races, new classes, new weapons and new spells. What more could you ask for?

Visit www.fastforwardgames.com to learn more about this and other upcoming products.

The Complete Monstrous Fighter's Compendium is a d20 System sourcebook that finally provides all of the rules necessary to create and play a monstrous Player Character. But it is far more! Though set in the Green Races campaign world of Elara, Players and GMs alike can use this book in any fantasy campaign where creatures like goblins, orcs and trolls wander the lands. Included also are rules for running these types of creatures as PCs and NPCs, as well as both base classes and prestige classes unique to these martially-minded races.

About the Authors:
Fast Forward Entertainment assembled an incredibly talented group of authors, including winners of the prestigious Origins Award, to take you inside the heads of your worst enemies. Together, Timothy Brown, Johnathan Cassie, Kurt Hausheer, David Lyons, W. Jason Peck, Jarad Fennell, M.K. McArtor, Gary McBride, Steve Miller, Christoffer Trossen and James M. Ward have designed a book sure to bring hundreds of hours of enjoyment to players and GMs who thought they knew all there was to know about these grim warriors!
