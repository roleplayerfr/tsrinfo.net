Item Code: #TG1001
Title: Bluffside: City on the Edge
Type: Accessory
Published: 2002
Publisher: Thunderhead Games
Format: 144-page perfect-bound book w/fold-out map
Civilization is still picking up the pieces from an asteroid strike hundreds of thousand of years ago. The epicenter? Bluffside. Only 200 years after being rediscovered, Bluffside is a boom town boasting the most precious metal in the known world adamantine. From the ancient ruins, to the vast Undercity, to the floating port of Sordadon, Bluffside: City on the Edge is a city that promises to become the home port for thousands of adventures. The first in our line of truly modular settings, Bluffside can be easily dropped into any d20 fantasy campaign world, or in conjunction with out other products.

Bluffside features the following:

  • New creatures
  • New prestige classes
  • New feats
  • Dozens of new magic items, spells, deities, and much, much more.
Plus, in support of Bluffside, players and GMs alike will find more on our website:

  • Original fiction based in Bluffside
  • Free downloadable maps and extras on a password protected page only available to purchasers of Bluffside: City on the Edge
  • News, updated regularly, written from within the city walls
  • Working in conjunction with the FANCC ( http://www.enworld.org/fancc), we offer all GMs and players a place to freely post their visions of Bluffside. From characters to trade routes to adventures, we are constructing a complete, approved haven for all Bluffside enthusiasts to make Bluffside truly their city. Players and GMs will not only be able to submit their Bluffside work, they will be able to freely download to work of others using the OGL. We plan for Bluffside to be the most thoroughly developed city ever. This netbook will help us immensely in reaching our goal.
