THE DEMON GOD...

A name so old and obscure, most people no longer remember the terror it conceals. Now, something evil has slithered its way into the village of Golden Lake, and into the minds and souls of the villagers. Has the old temple to the demon prince Ochremeshk become a threat again after so many centuries? And how can it be stopped when its location remains lost in plain sight?

The cultists of the demon god are out to get you. Track them down and wipe them out in their own lair in this challenging d20 adventure for 14th- and 15th-level characters. Demon God's Fane serves as a companion to the popular The Book of Eldritch Might, featuring many of that sourcebook's high magic prestige classes, feats, spells, magic items, and more.

Monte Cook, known for his previous adventures like Labyrinth of Madness, A Paladin in Hell, and Return to the Temple of Elemental Evil, always delivers unique and twisted new concepts of evil.

Malhavoc Press is Monte Cook's d20 System imprint devoted to publishing unusual magic, monsters, and evocative elements that go beyond traditional fantasy. Malhavoc Press products exhibit a mastery of the d20 System rules that only one of the game's original designers can offer.
