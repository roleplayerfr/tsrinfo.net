Sins of the Father

Strange dreams and terrible nightmares grip the common people of Leafton. Horrific images of burning flesh and unknown terrors haunt the town. Many disapear, never to be seen again. But at least the circus is in town...

Only True Heroes Can Stop
the Lord of Shadows

What Evil Lurks is an outdoor, wilderness, and dungeon adventure designed for 4-6 characters of 8th-10th level focused on roleplaying, culminating in the solution to a great mystery. It includes numerous NPCs, new spellcasting feats, and new monsters - including an evil monster template! Can your payers find out what is behind the evil dreams and missing villagers? Can they stop the soul engine before it unleashese darkness on the world?

A fantasy adventure for 4 to 6 players of 8th to 10th level.
