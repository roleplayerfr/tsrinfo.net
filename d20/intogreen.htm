Title: Into the Green: A Guide to Forests, Jungles, Woods, and Plains
Type: Accessory
Author: Thomas Knauss
Published: 2003
Publisher: Bastion Press
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
Into the Green provides rich detail for four different types of green environments, describing the ecology, terrain, hazards, and resources of each. From tall alpine forests to dark foreboding woods, from lonely and sprawling plains to dense and sweltering jungles, no feature is left out.

Into the Green is designed as a guidebook for both players and DMs alike, providing the resources needed to flesh out a wilderness campaign, and the knowledge needed to survive in one. For the DM, Into the Green details the living creatures and the unique conditions that make up each of the four terrains. Trees, plants, animals, microorganisms, monsters, pitfalls, and climates are all included - the complete cycle of life is defined.

Along with each of the environments are a wide variety of skills, substances, spells, concoctions, devices, gear, and information invaluable for dealing with the specific hazards of the wilds. Every plant tells a story, every creature provides a clue to success, and every monster is a treasure in and of itself.

Into the Green requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.
