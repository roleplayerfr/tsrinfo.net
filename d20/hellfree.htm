Item Code: #GRR1006
Title: Hell in Freeport
Type: Adventure
For: 10th-12th level characters
Author: Jim Bishop, Jess Lebow, and Paul Bazakas
Published: 2001
Publisher: Green Ronin Publishing
Format: 88-page perfect-bound book
Two cities, three acts, a thousand ways to die!

Freeport has withstood naval attacks, lethal magics, and forbidden cults, but how will it fare versus the forces of Hell? Two hundred years ago the army of the Inquisition silenced the infernal hordes on the island of Devil's Cry. Now something stirs amidst the ruins and Freeport needs heroes to ward off a new threat. This is the good fight to be sure, but the legions of Hell are not to be trifled with. Do you have what it takes to match swords and wits with Hell's elite?

Return to Freeport, the city of adventure, in this stand-alone scenario for characters of level 10-12. The rewards are great... for those who survive!

Hell in Freeport is 88 pages of non-stop action featuring:

A dazzling array of encounters and challenges.
A stunning eight-page map insert done in full color.
Two new monsters.
A special web enhancement with four new prestige classes.
Devils, devils, devils!

About Freeport: Freeport is an exciting fantasy city ready for use in any campaign. First seen in Chris Pramas's Origins Award nominated Death in Freeport, the city continued to grow in the rest of the Freeport Trilogy and a series of free, online articles. Stay tuned for the epic sourcebook, Secrets of Freeport, coming later this year from Green Ronin Publishing.

Hell in Freeport requires the use of the Dungeons and Dragons Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast for use.
