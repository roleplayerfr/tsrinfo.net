Title: Living Fantasy
Type: Accessory
Author: Gary Gyax
Published: 2003
Publisher: Troll Lord Games
Format: 160-page perfect-bound hardback book
Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons, Player's Handbook, Third Edition, published by Wizards of the Coast.

For the vigilant gamer or the driven world builder, Living Fantasy drives forward the host of information in the Gygaxian Fantasy Worlds series. Setting designers who master the knowledge within this tome shall breathe magic into their creation. From vagabond to king, Living Fantasy details the everyday life of those who inhabit the towns, cities, and castles in a fantasy world, and contains supplementary rules for the d20 System and the Lejendary Adventure system.
