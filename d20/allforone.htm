Defenders of the Realm

Religious warfare wracks France, nobles challenge the king's power and commoners question the way of things. In these times, the Kings of France must have protection: the elite soldiers known as Musketeers.

ALL FOR ONE lets your players enter the world of Alexander Dumas, or play in the reality in which he set his novels. Fight as a King's Musketeer or Cardinal's Guard, or conduct secret missions as an Intendant. New rules are included for gunpowder weapons and of course no Musketeer would be complete without panache. All the major characters of The Three Musketeers and The Man in the Iron Mask are present as well.
