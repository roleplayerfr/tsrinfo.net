ROUSING TALES OF BLOOD AND THUNDER!

Anything can happen in Freeport - and in this book, it does! Evil serpent folk, hidden pirate treasure, lost civilizations, foreign intrigue, scheming merchants, nightmarish creatures and marauding orcs are all in a day's work in the City of Adventure. Death lurks around every corner, but wealth and glory await those with the strength and the wits to survive. An adventure anthology for the ENnie and Origins Award winning city setting, Tales of Freeport includes:

  • The Soul of the Serpent: A perilous adventure into the underground world of the serpent folk!
  • Fair Salvage: An ancient and mysterious race revisits Freeport for the first time in two centuries!
  • Cut-Throat Gold: A treasure map leads to deadly peril amid the remains of a lost civilizatiom!
  • The Last Resort: Intrigue and action in Freeport's exclusive hotel!
  • Plots & Places: Two new locations and thirteen adventure ideas to expand and use!
  • Rules You Can Use: New skills, prestige classes, and additional rules for firearms!
Face down ancient horrors and evil magic with hot loead and cold steel, and woe be to the swab you dares water your rum! The Tales of Freeport are ready to be told!

Tales of Freeport requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons, Third Edition Core Books, published by Wizards of the Coast.
