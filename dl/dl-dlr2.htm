Item Code: #9344 (DLR2)
Title: Taladas: The Minotaurs
Author: Colin McComb
Type: Accessory
Published: 1991
Format: 64-page book
Awe-inspiring, brutish, militaristic, honor-bound: All these words and more describe the minotaurs of Taladas, the "other continent" on the world of Krynn.
This booklet exposes the secrets of minotaur society for the first time in the DRAGONLANCE campaign setting. Among the information within these pages are their origin myths, bureaucratic organizations, military strategies, and philosophical leanings. Major minotaur non-player characters are also delineated - including a magic-user and a paladin of Paladine! The Warriors' Guild is explored in detail, for use in your own campaign.
Prepare to be enlightened (and perhaps surprised!) as you read this, the first material ever released about these mysterious and misunderstood creatures: the minotaurs of Taladas.
