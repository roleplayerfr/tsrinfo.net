Item Code: #9400 (TM3)
Title: The World of Krynn Trail Map
Type: Accessory
Published: 1989
Format: Oversized fold-out map, cardstock folder/cover
This trail map presents the continent of Ansalon on the World of Krynn. From the elven stronghold of Silvanesti to the high plains of Palanthus, from Sancrist Isle to the realms of the minotaurs, all the locations of the DRAGONLANCE Chronicles are brought to life in a beautifully rendered format.

The maps are fully updated with information readily assembled for ease of reference and use on the trail.

A new format for a grand old world!
