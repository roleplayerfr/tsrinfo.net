Title: Stealth and Style: A Variant Class Guidebook to the Infiltrator and Basiran Dancer
Type: Accessory
Author: John Terra
Published: 2004
Format: 112-page perfect-bound book
It's all about motion...
Whether done to a steady, catchy beat or to the sounds of silence, motion applied in unique ways yields results undreamed and unthought of... Until now!

At last, infiltrators and Basiran dancers have their own book! Revealing their special secrets for the first time, this book is packed with useful game material such as:

  • The origins and history of these variant classes.
  • The secret code language of the infiltrator class
  • 10 new feats including urban tracking and double-jointed
  • 25 new spells such as dance of the bull, frenzy and quickstep
  • 11 new pieces of equipment
  • 18 new magic items
  • 12 new organizations for Basiran dancers and infiltrators
  • Details on role-playing motivation including how the Basiran dancer and infiltrator relate to other classes and races
  • 13 new prestige classes including the saboteur, death dancer and the elemental dancer
  • Details on NPC Basiran dancers and infiltrators that make great allies (or enemies)
Although usable in any campaign, these classes are especially suited for the Kingdoms of Kalamar Dungeons & Dragons campaign setting, a realistic, dynamic world where complex political alliances mix with marauding bands of humanoids, and medieval technology and culture come face to face with magic and the fantastic. Whatever type of adventure you seek, you can find it here.
With all this and more, this book is a valuable resource for anyone who wants to add intrigue and panache to their campaign, players and Dungeon Masters alike! To use this book in a Dungeons & Dragons campaign you also need a copy of the Player's Handbook, the Dungeon Master's Guide and the Monster Manual. This product utilizes material from the v.3.5 revision of the D&D game. You can also find further detail on the Kingdoms of Kalamar campaign setting in the Kingdoms of Kalamar Campaign Setting Sourcebook, the Kingdoms of Kalamar Player's Guide or any of our other fine supplements and adventures.
