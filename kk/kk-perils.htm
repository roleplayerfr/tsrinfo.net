Title: Perils of Pekal
Type: Adventure
For: 1st level characters
Author: Daniel S. Donnelly
Published: 2005
Format: 80-page perfect-bound book
This book contains three separate Dungeons & Dragons adventures set in the Kingdoms of Kalamar campaign setting.
These scenarios serve as an excellent introduction both to the world and to the Living Kingdoms of Kalamar setting detailed in the Pekal Gazetteer.
The adventure begins in the town of Lebolegido where the characters are called upon to aid a small farming community that has fallen on hard times.
From there, the characters can travel Lake Eb'Sobet aboard the vessel the Heart's Home. Many fishermen have disappeared and it it up to them to solve the mystery once and for all.
The final scenario brings the adventurers to the capital of Pekal, Bet Rogala. The College of Magic is hiring but a simple errand can prove complicated indeed!

These adventures provide ample role-play oppurtunities for novice and veteran players alike. In addition, numerous gorgeous maps and background information make this a must-have for any Dungeon Master.
