Item Code: #749
Title: Psionics
Type: Accessory
Author: Nigel D. Findley
Published: 1991
Format: 128-page perfect-bound book
Since the dawn of time, man has dreamed of having power over the universe around him...

This sourcebook makes this dream a reality.

Psionics includes: Over 100 new spells; new magic items; give highly detailed psionicist sub-classes, including: Somniomancers, Telepaths, Telekineticists, Pyromancers, and Empaths; and adventure ideas that any Game Master can easily augment and alter to fit his gaming needs.
