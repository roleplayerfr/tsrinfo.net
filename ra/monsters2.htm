Item Code: #746
Title: Monsters of Myth and Legend II
Type: Accessory
Author: Laurel Nicholson and John Keefe
Published: 1989(?)
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
Monsters of Myth and Legend II, the companion volume to Monsters of Myth and Legend, is a must for anyone who has ever wanted to adventure in exotic lands around the world. Whether you have an avid interest in the legends, lore, and tales of different countries, or you want to add variety and intrigue to your campaign or adventure, Monsters of Myth and Legend II is an outstanding work to add to your fantasy role-playing library.

This sourcebook features details of mythological beings from the ancient cultures of:
Africa
Central and South America
The Eskimos
Japan
The Middle East

Each description in this compendium contains statistics and detailed background of the being and the muth on which it is based, as well as all appropriate game-related information to complete your campaign setting. Also included is a listing of special items and new spells which can be used to further enhance your adventuring experiences in faraway lands.
