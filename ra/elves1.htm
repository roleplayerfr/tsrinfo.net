Item Code: #711
Title: Elves
Type: Accessory
Author: Cheron Fitzgerald Carr & Delbert Carr, Jr.
Published: 1st print - 1983
Format: 96-page perfect-bound book
The dance of death to the Dark god has begun. Even the sunlight wanes...

In six complete and related adventures, your hardy band travels into the homelands of each of the six Elven races. The quest is to recover the pieces of an ancient artifact. Without it the high elves will never be able to return the dark god to his bleak plane before he dominates yours.

Within each adventure there is a detailed background of the culture, magic, religion, and history of the Elven races.

ELVES

In ELVES you get information on:

High Elves Ice Elves
Dark Elves Demi-Elves
Wood Elves Grey Elves

plus:

Eleven Cities and Towns

ELVES is a fantasy roleplaying module for four to eight players from fourth to seventh level. Suitable for use with all popular roleplaying systems including Advanced Dungeons and Dragons*
