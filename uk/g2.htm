Item Code: #TSRG2 (G2)
Title: The Glacial Rift of the Frost Giant Jarl
Type: Adventure
Author: Gary Gygax
Published: 1978
Format: 8-page book w/cover
This module contains background information, referee's notes, two level maps, and exploration matrix keys. It provides a complete module for play of ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS, and it can be use alone or as the second of a three-part expedition adventure which also employs DUNGEON MODULE G1 (STEADING OF THE HILL GIANT CHIEF) and DUGEON MODULE G3 ( HALL OF THE FIRE GIANT KING).
