Item Code: #TSRD3 (D3)
Title: Vault of the Drow
Type: Adventure
Author: Gary Gygax
Published: 1978
Format: 32-page book w/tri-fold cover
This module contains background information, a large-scale referee's mao with a matching partial map for players, referee's notes, special exploration and encounter pieces, a hex map detailing an enormous cavern area, a special temple map, encounter and map matrix keys, and additional sections pertaining to unique new creatures for use with this module and the game as a whole. A complete setting for play of ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS is contained herein. This module can be played alone, as the final part of a series of three modules (with DESCENT INTO THE DEPTHS OF THE EARTH, D1 and SHRINE OF THE KUO-TOA, D2.), or as the sixth part of a continuing series of modules which form a special campaign scenario (DUNGEON MODULES G2, G2, G3, D1, D2, D3, and Q1, QUEEN OF THE DEMONWEB PITS, soon to be released).

If you have found this module and its companions exciting, stay tuned for more action from the Game Wizards!
