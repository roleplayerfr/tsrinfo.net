Item Code: #88686
Title: Dungeon Master's Screen
Type: Accesory
Published: 2002
Format: 32-page book, four-panel cardstock screen, cover
Become the Mightiest Force in Faerûn

From behind this four-panel screen, you control all that takes place in your FORGOTTEN REALMS campaign. Featuring stunning new art, this invaluable game aid contains key tables from the Player's Handbook, DUNGEON MASTER's Guide, and FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting - and helps you guard your notes, die rolls, and other surprises from your player's eyes. Also included is a useful 32-page booklet filled with random encounter tables for dungeon levels 1 to 20, and wilderness encounters for over 30 climate and terrain combinations.

To use this accessory, you also need the FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting, the Player's Handbook, the DUNGEON MASTER's Guide, and the Monster Manual.
