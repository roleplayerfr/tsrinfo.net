Title: Nurture and Nature
Type: Adventure
For: 4th level characters
Author: Eric Menge
Published: 2004
Format: 28-page book
Raiders of a Different Stripe

Caravans are being attacked, and this time the assailants aren't goblins, bugbears, or other minions of the Hark. The Half-Elven Renegades have attacked a convoy of wagons, and taken sun elves hostage. Can you find the half-elves hidden stronghold and save the sun elves from a fate worse than death?

Nurture and Nature is an adventure for the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game. It can either be played as a stand-alone adventure, or as part of the LEGACY OF THE GREEN REGENT campaign offered by the RPGA. Designed to challenge 4th-level D&D heroes, this adventure pits them against a twisted foe using half-elf bandits to further its own twisted ends.

To use this adventure, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, the DUNGEON MASTER'S Guide and the Monster Manual. This adventure is set in the FORGOTTEN REALMS game world, and it may also be useful, but not necessary to also have a copy FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting.
