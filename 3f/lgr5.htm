Title: Dark Exodus
Type: Adventure
For: 3rd level characters
Author: Christopher Lindsay
Published: 2004
Format: 24-page book
Under the Dark Canopy

Powers in Loudwater are searching for the reasons behind the orc migrations. But expeditions into the vast High Forest will uncover more than just the movements or orcs. Dark things move in the forest, searching for egress.

Dark Exodus is an adventure for the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game. It can either be played as a stand-alone adventure, or as part of the LEGACY OF THE GREEN REGENT campaign offered by the RPGA. Designed to challenge 3rd-level D&D heroes, in this adventure they face the strange dangers that dwell in the great expanse of the High Forest.

To use this adventure, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, the DUNGEON MASTER'S Guide and the Monster Manual. This adventure is set in the FORGOTTEN REALMS game world, and it may also be useful, but not necessary to also have a copy FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting.
