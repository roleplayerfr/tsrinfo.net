Title: Sons of Gruumsh
Type: Adventure
For: 4th level characters
Author: Christopher Perkins
Published: 2005
Format: 32-page book
No One Escapes the Wrath of Gruumsh

Trouble brews in Thar as orc tribes converge on the ruined fortress of Xûl-Jarak, flocking to the banner of a charismatic warlord. What are their wicked intentions, and what connection do they have to six missing nobles from Melvaunt?

This stand-alone DUNGEONS & DRAGONS adventure is designed for 4th-level characters. Although set in the world of Faerûn, it can easily be placed in any D&D campaign. To run this adventure, you need the Player's Handbook, Dungeon Master's Guide, Monster Manual, and FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting. The adventure can be played using D&D Miniatures.
