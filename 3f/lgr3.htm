Title: Key to Phantoms' Cloister
Type: Adventure
For: 2nd level characters
Author: Ving Domanski
Published: 2004
Format: 28-page book
Strange Things Afoot in the City

A strange visitation on the streets of Loudwater leads to the search for what's behind an ancient spectral mystery.

Key to Phantom's Cloister is an adventure for the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game. It can either be played as a stand-alone adventure, or as part of the LEGACY OF THE GREEN REGENT campaign offered by the RPGA. Designed to challenge 2nd-level D&D heroes, in this adventure they are asked to uncover what is behind an ancient and ghostly mystery.

To use this adventure, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, the DUNGEON MASTER'S Guide and the Monster Manual. This adventure is set in the FORGOTTEN REALMS game world, and it may also be useful, but not necessary to also have a copy FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting.
