Welcome to the Original World of the D&D Game!

This special limited edition, brought to you by the RPGA Network, provides the first look at the world of the new DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game, setting the stage for the RPGA's LIVING GREYHAWK campaign - the most ambitious shared-world endeavor in the history of tabletop gaming.

Inside is everything you need to launch your own version of the first campaign world:

  • A full-color map of the land of the Flanaess, also showing the continents and seas of the planet Oerth
  • A short history of the land
  • Descriptions of 60 countries, ready for adventure
