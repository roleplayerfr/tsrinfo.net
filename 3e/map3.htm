Title: Map Folio 3-D
Type: Accessory
Author: Dennis Kauth and Todd Gamble
Published: 2005
Format: 32 cardstock pages, cover
SET THE SCENE FOR ADVENTURE

Inside you'll discover intimidating walls, comfortable cottages, a bustling smithy, and other three-dimensional structures. This accessory for the D&D game provides a small village of highly detailed card-stock buildings, walls, and other structures for you to assemble and use in any game. Scaled for use with DUNGEONS & DRAGONS Miniatures, each structure may be used individually or combined with others. Full-color exteriors enhance your game and capture your players' imagination.
