UNLOCK A WELLSPRING OF KNOWLEDGE

This supplement presents an inexhaustible source of new rules to introduce into your DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game. Inside are ideas, options, and alternatives to the standard D&D rules you can choose from to fit your campaign's style of play. From variant classes, races, feats, and abilities to alternate spellcasting options, Unearthed Arcana offers a tremendous array of material for you to explore.

To use this supplement, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, Dungeon Master's Guide, and Monster Manual.
A player needs only the Player's Handbook.
