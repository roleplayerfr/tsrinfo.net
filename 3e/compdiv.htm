WIELD POWER

GRANTED BY THE GODS

Legends tell of brave champions whose unwavering fiath earned them the favor of the gods. Empowered by the might and magic of their deity, these devoted characters trascend the realm of mere heroes.

This supplement for the D&D game provides everything you need to create divinely inspired characters of any class. Along with new base classes, prestige classes, feats, spells, magic items, and relics, Complete Divine also provdes guidelines for incorporating religion - from mysterious cults to powerful theocracies - into your campaign.

To use this supplement, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, Dungeon Master's Guide, and Monster Manual.
A player needs only the Player's Handbook.
