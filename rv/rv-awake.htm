Item Code: #9452
Title: The Awakening
Type: Adventure
Author: Lisa Smedman
Published: 1994
Format: 64-page book w/cover, fold-out map
Upon the horse plains of Nova Vaasa, the Koshka Bluffs rise from the earth like gigantic, misshapen tumors. They give up stone to feed the sheer walls of Castle Faerhaaven, but lately they have yielded stranger objects: figurines, coins, and other odd artifacts, which curse those foolish enough to claim them.
Now, the ancient priestess Sachmet has awakened from a timeless sleep to come and take them back....
