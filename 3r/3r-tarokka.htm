Title: Tarokka Deck
Type: Accessory
Published: 2003
Publisher: Arthaus / Sword & Sorcery Studios
Format: 66 accessory cards
A Tarot-like deck based on the imagery of Wizards of the Coast's Ravenloft campaign setting. For use as a game accessory, or for fortunetelling or for collectors of Tarot-like decks.

This 68-card color Tarokka deck contains:

  • 14 Major Arcana cards
  • 40 Minor Arcana cards
  • Easy-to-use instruction cards
  • Variant layouts explained
  • Ideas for use in game and out
