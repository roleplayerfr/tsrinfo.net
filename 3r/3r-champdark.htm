The Ravenloft campaign setting is an officially licensed Dungeons & Dragons property.

The Darkness Within

Nemeses of good, yet puppets of evil, the beleagured men and women who serve the Dark Powers may achieve their own terrible grandeur in the Dread Realms - or they may somehow find their way back to the light. In any other world, they might be considered villains. In Ravenloft, they are champions.
Inside this book are rules for creating and developing anti-heroic characters, as well as prestige classes, information on the dark religions and secret societies in the Land of Mists and ideas for running campaigns.
