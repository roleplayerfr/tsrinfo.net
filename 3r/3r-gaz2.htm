The Ravenloft campaign setting is an officially licensed Dungeons & Dragons property.

Legacies of Terror, Lands of Legend
From the towering forests of Falkovnia, clutched in the iron fist of the mercenary-king Vlad Drakov, to the efficient and rationally ruled realm of dour Lamordia, intrigue and terror hold sway. Secret police and elite soldiers enforce their darklords' rule with brutal efficiency. Yet such fanatics are feared less than the undead horrors stalking the streets of Necropolis... and their cruel order is preferred to the ambitious plans of Darkon's enigmatic Azalin Rex. In these northern lands, steeped in magic and warped by the mad desire for conquest, evil never sleeps.
The second in a series of setting sourcebooks, Ravenloft Gazetteer II examines the northernmost regions of the central continent. Within these pages, the feudal domain of the wizard-king Azalin Rex, the damned city of Necropolis, the republican aristocracy of Lamordia and the tyrannical military realm of Falkovnia await the bold-hearted and the daring.
