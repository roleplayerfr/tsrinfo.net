Title: Lands of Conflict
Type: Accessory
Published: 2004
Publisher: Arthaus / Sword & Sorcery Studios
Format: 200-page perfect-bound hardback book
Return to the East!

Undead march uncontested across the plains of Lordaeron and Alliance remnants struggle for survival in this campaign setting sourcebook for the Dungeons & Dragons Warcraft the Roleplaying Game, which translates the hugely successful Warcraft computer game series into an exciting pen-and-paper roleplaying game world based on the popular revised d20 System rules!

Requires the use of the Dungeons & Dragons Player's Handbook, published by Wizards of the Coast. This product utilizes updated material from the v.3.5 revision

Designed with extensive input from the creative minds behind the Warcraft computer games, Lands of Conflict includes:

  • An extensive look at campaigning in earlier eras of Warcraft's history, along with an updated and thorough timeline.
  • Descriptions of all major regions on the eastern continents of Azeroth, Khaz Modan and Lordaeron, along with stats for prominent NPCs and for mass combat units.
  • Three adventures set in the eastern continents to kickstart your Warcraft RPG game.
  • Eight fully detailed organizations, including the Cult of the Damned, the Explorers' Guild and the Scarlet Crusade.
  • Four new prestige classes, including the dark apothecary and Defias renegade; six new monsters, such as the dragonhawk and worgen; new feats; and new equipment and magic items!


Originally solicited under the title Lands of Azeroth.
