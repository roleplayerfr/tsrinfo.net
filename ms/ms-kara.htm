Item Code: #2500
Title: Karameikos: Kingdom of Adventure
Type: Campaign Setting
Author:Jeff Grubb, Aaron Allston and Thomas M. Reid
Published: 1994
Format: 128-page perfect-bound book, 32-page book, 12 accessory sheets, 8 cardstock hero cards, 2 fold-out maps, CD with jewel case
Karameikos ... a classic fantasy setting where bold adventurers can become legendary heroes.


Whether you have played only the FIRST QUEST audio CD game or have enjoyed the AD&D game for years, you'll find high adventure in the Kingdom of Karameikos. Discover a magical land sure to challenge the bravest hearts and sharpest swords. Hear this realm come to life in two exciting quests featuring dramatic sounds on compact disc!

Inside you'll discover ...
An interactive audio compact disc featuring thrilling sound effects, character voices, music for two adventures
A 32-page Adventure Book compatible with the rules of the FIRST QUEST audio CD game and the AD&D game Player's Handbook and DUNGEON MASTER Guide.
A 128-page Explorer's Guide, lavishly illustrated in full-color
2 full-color poster size maps
12 parchment maps, illustrations, and player props
8 full-color hero cards.

See the wonders of Karameikos, from the deadly Fort Doom to King's Stefan castle. Study at the School of Magecraft. Clash swords with the noble Knights of the Griffon. Match wits with nefarious thieves of the Iron Ring. Uncover mysteries of the past, including the jackal-headed Hutaakans... and much more!
