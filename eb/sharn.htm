Item Code: #86420
Title: Sharn: City of Towers
Type: Accessory
Author: Keith Baker and James Wyatt
Published: 2004
Format: 192-page perfect-bound hardback book, CD
DISCOVER A CITY FILLED WITH ENDLESS ADVENTURE

Hop aboard a skycoach and hurl yourself into adventure amid the soaring towers of Khorvaire's most spectacular city. Plunge into perilous aerial battles. Embark on deadly expeditions throught the bowels of the city. For those bold enough to seek it, the City of Towers promises limitless excitement, danger, fame, and fortune.

Inside this book, you'll find all the details you'll need to explore the awe-inspiring city of Sharn - a cornerstone of the EBERRON campaign setting:

  • Descriptions of over 100 city districts
  • More than a dozen new guilds and organizations with over 30 fully developed NPCs ranging from specific individuals to generic citizens
  • New feats, spells, prestige classes, magic items, and monsters
  • Detailed maps of Sharn and various locations around the city.

    To use this supplement, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, Dungeon Master's Guide, Monster Manual, and EBERRON Campaign Setting. A player needs only the Player's Handbook and EBERRON Campaign Setting.
