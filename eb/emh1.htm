Title: Reflections of the Multiverse
Type: Adventure
For: 4-5 1st-level characters
Published: 2005
Format: 32-page book
Death From Beyond

A strange death in the halls of the Diggers' Union hall in Wroat, leads to a strange conspiracy foretold by a long-dead gnome academic. Can you piece the mystery together before an otherworldly threat is unleashed upon Eberron?

Reflections of the Multiverse is an adventure for the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game. It can either be played as a stand-alone adventure, or as part of the MARK OF HEROES campaign offered by the RPGA. Designed to challenge 1st-level D&D heroes, it sets the characters on the trail of a killer mysterious killer with ties beyond the known universe.

To use this adventure, a Dungeon Master also needs the Player's Handbook, the DUNGEON MASTER'S Guide and the Monster Manual. This adventure is set in the Eberron game world, and it may also be useful, but not necessary, to also have a copy Eberron Campaign Setting.
