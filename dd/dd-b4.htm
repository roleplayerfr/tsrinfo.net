Item Code: #9049 (B4)
Title: The Lost City
Type: Adventure
Author: Tom Moldvay
Published: 1982
Format: 28-page book, tri-fold cover, three-hole punched
Lost in the desert! The only hope for survival is a ruined city rising out of the sands. Food, water, and wealth await heroic adventurers inside an ancient pyramid ruled by a strange race of masked beings.

This module includes a cover folder with maps, and a descriptive booklet with a ready-made adventure for the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS Basic game. It also includes enough information to continue the adventure beyond level 3, using the DUNGEONS & DRAGONS Expert game rules.
