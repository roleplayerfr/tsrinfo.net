Item Code: #8007
Title: Hexagonal Mapping Booklet
Type: Accessory
Published: 1981
Format: 128-page book
This book contains approximately 128 pages of 7/32" hex paper printed on both sides, usable for games and game campaigns. The hex sheets can be used for any number of purposes: creation of game and campaign maps, mapping during play, movement plotting, and record-keeping.
