Item Code: #2107 (MC5)
Title: Monstrous Compendium Appendix
Type: Accessory
Published: 1990
Format: 32 loose five-hole punched pages, 4 cardstock dividers, folder
Prepare yourselves and your characters....Here come more monsters, this time from the GREYHAWK Campaign Setting! These 64 pages are filled with beasties and creatures, from aspis to zygon - and everything in-between! Crystalmist, dragons (yes, there are more - four more to be precise), and plenty of deadly plants await entry into your campaign. As always, these monster descriptions are organized into 64 5-hole punched pages, ready to be incorporated into you Monstrous Compendium binder. And, you get four full-color dividers with identification tabs! Snap them in, and set them free!
