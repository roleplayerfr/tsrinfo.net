Title: The Catacombs of the Necromancers
Type: Accessory
Published: 1998
Format: Single sheet, double-sided
The Map
A few coppers buys this scrap of parchment bearing a map on one side and the text in the box below on the other. The lettering is done with a clear, steady hand. The peddler who offers it makes no claims about its authenticity and values it strictly as a curiousity.
In any case, the name Didro Eightfingers (from the map on the reverse of this sheet) is familiar to your character. Didro is a luckless thief who has appeared in several short plays. Perhaps this is merely an old theatrical handbill.

For use with the Return to the Tomb of Horrors Adventure
