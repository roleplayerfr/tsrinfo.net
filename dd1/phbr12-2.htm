Title: The Complete Paladin's Handbook
Type: Accessory
Published: 2nd print(?) - 1994
Format: 128-page perfect-bound book
In the fight against evil, the paladin stands as Good's supreme, undaunted champion. This 128-page accessory helps you play the noblest warriors of the AD&D game. Create or enhance your paladin with new proficiencies and equipment, new rules for bonded mounts, and paladin kits like the divinate, envoy, ghosthunter, skyrider, and the indominatble wyrmslayer. The Complete Paladin's Handbook attends the world's finest heroes as they achieve their lofty ideals.
