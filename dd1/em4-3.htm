Title: Encyclopedia Magica Volume Four
Type: Accessory
Published: 3rd print - 1999
Format: 416-page perfect-bound book
The Encyclopedia Magica is a four-volume series that describes every magical item for the AD&D game. These items have been collected from rule books, campaign settings, supplements, adventures, and magazines dating back to 1975. This essential reference tool puts the power of more than 20 years of AD&D history at your fingertips! This volume also contains an exhaustive index for the entire series.
