Item Code: #2108
Title: Legends & Lore
Type: Accessory
Author: James M. Ward and Troy Denning
Published: 2nd print(?) - 1990
Format: 192-page perfect-bound hardcover book
Add the grandeur of epic adventure to your AD&D game! Legends & Lore is a reference for use with the AD&D 2nd Edition Rules. It describes dozens of mythological deities, heroes, and monsters from 11 different cultures.
Historical attention has been paid to the priests who worshipped the deities. Each entry outlines the powers granted to followers of specific faiths. In addition, you'll learn about the ancient cultures and their spells, items of magic, and character classes. Legends & Lore is a vital historical reference book for the serious AD&D game player!
