Item Code: IQ3
Title: Warriors of the Gray Queen
Type: Adventure
Author: Jeff Grubb
Published: 1999
Format: 16-page mini-book
Warriors of the Gray Queen is an adventure using the Advanced Dungeons & Dragons game system, for low-level heroes. It is a prelude to the upcoming Return to the Keep on the Borderlands adventure from TSR, but Warriors of the Gray Queen is not required to play Return. This adventure may be played as a seperate adventure or as part of a larger unrelated campaign.
