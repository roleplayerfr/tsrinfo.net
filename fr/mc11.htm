Item Code: #2125 (MC11)
Title: Monstrous Compendium Forgotten Realms Appendix
Type: Accessory
Published: 1991
Format: 32 loose five-hole punched pages, 4 cardstock dividers, folder
Here it is - the second Monstrous Compenium Appendix for the FORGOTTEN REALMS campaign world!
In these pages you will meet the strikingly beautiful Ihiannan shee, the fingernail-sized monkey spider, and the nightmarish feyr. These and other brand-new, custom-made monsters (and a few updated favorites) lurk within this booklet, waiting for you to unleash them into your own campaign. As always, the pages are 5-hole punched for easy insertion into your own ring binder.
