Item Code: #2104
Title: Monstrous Compendium Forgotten Realms Appendix
Type: Accessory
More monsters escape from the Realms and into your AD&D 2nd Edition game! Sixty-four pages chock-full of new monsters for use in your own campaigns and with the FORGOTTEN REALMS Campaign Setting. From the gnomish svirfneblin to the deadly dracolich; from the wemic to the revenant; they're all here! Battle the beholder-kin of the North, the dinosaurs of the Jungles of Chult, and the oriental dragons of Kara-Tur. All these creatures and more are organized in 64 5-hole punched pages that will make a handy appendix to your Volume One Monstrous Compendium binder. Add them in and turn them loose on your players!
