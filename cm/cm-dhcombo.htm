Drazen's Horde
Drazen has forged a savage army from among the humanoid tribes of the southlands beyond the Blasted Desert. They have decimated all opposition in their native land and seek richer spoils in the north. Drazen is coming, and he's bringing a storm with him.

The Dungeons & Dragons Chainmail miniatures game is based on the d20 system and features some of the most infamous creatures the D&D universe has ever known.

These miniatures can also be used with the Dungeons & Dragons roleplaying game.
