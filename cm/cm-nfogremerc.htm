Item Code: #88585
Title: Ogre Mercenary
Faction: Neutral
Published: 2001
Need some muscle for your warband? The Limited Edition Ogre Mercenary doesn't have much in the brains department, but his axe will cut a swath through your enemies. Best of all, he's neutral, which means you can add him as a cross-faction troop to any warband. So grab this Limited Edition figure while you can, because it's being offered only at the launch of the Dungeons & Dragons Chainmail miniatures game.
