Title: The New Adventures of Fafhrd and Gray Mouser
Type: Campaign Setting
Published: 1996
Format: 96-page book, 64-page book, 32-page book, bi-fold cardstock screen, fold-out map
LANKHMAR - city of thieves, city of adventure. Home to FAFHRD and the GRAY MOUSER, the greatest adventurers in the world of Newhon.

Fritz Leiber's classic characters and world spring to life in this stand-alone role-playing game. Featuring a streamlined set of rules that were designed expressly for LANKHMAR adventures, yet are fully compatible with the ADVANCED DUNGEONS & DRAGONS game, this boxed set lets two or more players invent their own tales of high adventure in the winding, shadowy streets of Lankhmar. It includes three books: an easy-to-read rulebook, a guide to creating exciting adventures in the world of Newhon, and a pre-made adventure, plus a two-sided, full-color poster map of the City of LANKHMAR and the world of NEWHON.

Even if you haven't yet read the Lankhmar stories and novels of Fritz Leiber, this set will provide countless hours of roguish enjoyment. To play this game, you need at least one set of polyhedral dice (4-, 6-, 8-, 10-, 12-, and 20-sided dice). They are available where you found this game, and at hobby shops everywhere.
